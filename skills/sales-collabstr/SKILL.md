## Step 3 — Collabstr platform reference

### How Collabstr works

Collabstr is a **self-serve creator marketplace** — different from discovery-first platforms like Modash or CreatorIQ:

1. **Brands search creators** — browse 250K+ vetted influencers and UGC creators filtered by niche, platform, location, and follower count
2. **View transparent pricing** — every creator lists upfront pricing for content packages (posts, reels, videos, UGC)
3. **Purchase directly** — place an order for a specific package or negotiate a custom offer
4. **Escrow protection** — payment is held safely until you approve the deliverables
5. **OR post campaigns** — create campaign briefs and let creators apply with their pricing

This **marketplace + campaign model** means you can either browse and buy directly (fastest for one-off needs) or post campaigns for inbound applications (better for larger projects).

### Creator search and discovery

Browse and filter Collabstr's vetted creator network:

- **Platform filter** — Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
- **Content type** — sponsored posts, UGC, reels, stories, videos
- **Niche/category** — beauty, fitness, tech, food, fashion, lifestyle, gaming, and more
- **Location** — filter by country or region
- **Follower count** — nano, micro, mid-tier, macro
- **Pricing** — sort and filter by content package price
- **Creator profiles** — portfolio, social stats, past work, verified status

Every creator is vetted by the Collabstr team — identity verified and social media audited for fake followers and engagement quality.

### Campaign posting

Post a campaign brief visible to Collabstr's 700K+ creator network:

- **Campaign brief** — describe what you need, brand requirements, content expectations
- **Targeting** — specify demographics including niche, location, and follower count
- **Creator applications** — targeted creators submit their pricing for you to choose
- **Centralized assets** — upload images and brand guidelines in the campaign brief
- **Multiple campaigns** — Pro and Premium plans allow unlimited campaigns

### Orders and transactions

- **Placing an order** — purchase a creator's preset package or request a custom offer
- **Order modifications** — adjust price or deadline after order creation
- **Content submissions** — review deliverables, request revisions
- **Cancellation** — request cancellations with escrow protection (funds returned if work not started)
- **Escrow payment** — money held until you approve the creator's work

### Performance tracking

- **Analytics dashboard** — track Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube content in real time
- **Metrics** — impressions, engagement, reach tracked from a single dashboard
- **Content performance** — analyze performance over time across campaigns

### Team management

- **Team members** — invite team members to your Collabstr account
- **Workspace permissions** — manage access levels for different team members

### Bring Your Own Creators

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and pricing that may be outdated.*

- **10% marketplace fee on Basic plan adds up fast.** If you're spending $3K+/mo on creators, the Pro plan ($299/mo with reduced fees) or Premium ($399/mo at 5%) pays for itself. Calculate your breakeven before committing to a plan.
- **No native Shopify or ecommerce integration.** Unlike Modash or Afluencer, Collabstr doesn't sync with your Shopify store for product gifting. All product coordination happens outside the platform.
- **No API.** There's no public API for programmatic access, bulk operations, or CRM integration. Everything is done through the UI.
- **No built-in affiliate tracking.** Collabstr is transactional (pay per deliverable), not performance-based. If you want affiliate/commission-based creator partnerships, use Modash, LTK, or Creator.co instead.
- **Creator pool is smaller than discovery platforms.** 250K+ vetted creators vs Modash (350M+) or Influencity (200M+). The tradeoff is curation quality vs volume — Collabstr creators are vetted, but your niche may have limited options.
- **Limited analytics compared to dedicated platforms.** No EMV calculation, no ROAS tracking, no fake follower scoring beyond initial vetting. For deep campaign analytics, you'll need to supplement with another tool.
- **Regional coverage gaps.** Some users report limited creator availability outside the US. Check creator availability in your target market before committing.
- **Some creators may underdeliver.** Trustpilot reviews mention cases of paid influencers delivering zero clicks. Always review a creator's portfolio and past work carefully. Start with a small test order before committing to larger campaigns.
- **UI can feel slow.** G2 reviewers note the portal can be laggy. Not a dealbreaker but worth knowing.

## Troubleshooting

### Creator delivered content but it doesn't match the brief
**Symptom**: Received deliverables that don't align with what you asked for — wrong messaging, poor quality, or off-brand
**Cause**: Unclear brief, mismatched expectations, or creator didn't follow instructions
**Solution**: Use the content revision feature to request specific changes before approving the order. Be extremely specific in your initial brief — include mood boards, example content, exact talking points, and brand guidelines. For future orders, review the creator's portfolio to verify their style matches your needs before purchasing. Funds stay in escrow until you approve — you're protected.

### Campaign posted but no creators are applying
**Symptom**: Campaign has been live for days but zero or very few applications
**Cause**: Brief is too vague, compensation is below market rate, targeting is too narrow, or niche has limited creators
**Solution**: Check your compensation against Collabstr's average rates (micro-influencer posts average ~$198). Make the brief specific and appealing — creators skip vague campaigns. Broaden targeting (wider location, larger follower range). Add sample content to show what you're looking for. If your niche is very narrow, supplement with direct creator search and ordering instead of relying on campaign applications.

### Paid an influencer but got zero engagement or traffic
**Symptom**: Content was posted but generated no measurable results (0 clicks, minimal engagement)
**Cause**: Wrong audience fit, low-quality followers despite vetting, content didn't resonate, or no tracking in place
**Solution**: For future campaigns, always include UTM-tagged links or unique promo codes so you can track attribution. Review the creator's engagement rate and comment quality (not just follower count) before ordering. Start with small test orders ($50-$200) to validate a creator's audience before scaling spend. Use the analytics dashboard to track impressions and engagement on each piece of content.
