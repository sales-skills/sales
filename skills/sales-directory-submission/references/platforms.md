## GetMoreBacklinks

**Model**: Done-for-you managed service
**URL**: https://www.getmorebacklinks.org
**Has platform skill**: Yes (`/sales-getmorebacklinks`)

| Plan | Price | Directories | Delivery |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $87 | 50 | 14 working days |
| Advanced | $127 | 100 | 14 working days |
| Business | $187 | 200 (hand-pick) | 14 working days |
| Pro | $247 | 200 + 5 blog posts | 14 working days |

**Strengths**: Established service, DR 30+ guarantee within 45 working days, blog post writing on Pro tier.
**Weaknesses**: No API or dashboard, communication via email only, mixed online trust scores (ScamAdviser/ScamDoc), you don't choose directories on Starter/Advanced.
**Best for**: Founders who want fully hands-off bulk submissions and don't need control over directory selection.

## LaunchDirectories

**Model**: Done-for-you manual submissions, tailored per product
**URL**: https://launchdirectories.com

| Plan | Price | Directories | Delivery |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $119 | 30+ | 4-6 days |
| Pro | $169 | 60+ | 4-6 days |
| Premium | $219 | 100+ | 4-6 days |

**Strengths**: 100% manual submissions, directory list tailored to product niche, full report with live links + screenshots + credentials, fast 4-6 day delivery, claims average DR increase of 25 points across 100+ clients.
**Weaknesses**: Per-product pricing (no unlimited option), relatively new service, comparison content on their blog ranks themselves.
**Best for**: Founders who want quality over quantity with a curated, product-specific directory list.

## ListingBott

**Model**: Hybrid AI-automated + manual review, gradual rollout
**URL**: https://listingbott.com

| Plan | Price | Directories | Delivery |
|---|---|---|---|
| Early Adopter | $299 | 100 (from 10K+ database) | 4-5 weeks |
| Standard | $499 | 100 (from 10K+ database) | 4-5 weeks |
| Post-beta | $999 | 100 (from 10K+ database) | 4-5 weeks |

**Strengths**: AI selects best directories from 10K+ database based on goals, gradual 4-5 week rollout avoids spam signals, hybrid approach (automation + human review) balances speed and quality.
**Weaknesses**: Most expensive option, 100 submissions regardless of tier (pricing is about access timing not volume), slow delivery compared to competitors.
**Best for**: Founders with larger budgets who prioritize natural-looking submission patterns and AI-driven directory matching.

## StartupSubmit

**Model**: Done-for-you manual submissions
**URL**: https://startupsubmit.app

**Pricing**: Not publicly detailed in tiers — one-time payment, reportedly competitive.

**Strengths**: 250+ directory coverage (largest claimed count), 100% manual submissions (zero bots), 7-14 day delivery, satisfaction guarantee (will work until promised submission count is met), strong Trustpilot reviews.
**Weaknesses**: Pricing not transparent on the site, 250+ directories likely includes many low-authority sites, per-product pricing.
**Best for**: Founders who want maximum breadth of submissions with manual quality.

## AutoSaaSLaunch

**Model**: Self-serve Chrome extension + API automation
**URL**: https://autosaaslaunch.com

| Plan | Price | Products | Directories |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lifetime | $29 one-time | Unlimited | 18 (expanding to 30+) |

**Strengths**: Cheapest option by far ($29 unlimited products), you maintain control (submit through your own accounts), Chrome extension auto-fills forms, dashboard tracks submissions, instant real-time submissions.
**Weaknesses**: Only 18 directories currently (smallest coverage), requires your time to initiate submissions, Chrome extension model means you're still doing the work (just faster), not truly "done for you."
**Best for**: Technical founders, serial launchers, and agencies who want control and unlimited product support at minimal cost.

## SubmitJuice

**Model**: Done-for-you manual submissions
**URL**: https://www.submitjuice.com

**Pricing**: Starts at ~$29-99 (varies by package — free, classic, premium directory tiers).

**Strengths**: Manual submissions (no bots), 100+ directories, Airtable-based tracking dashboard, mix of dofollow and nofollow for natural backlink profile, completes submissions within 1 week.
**Weaknesses**: Smaller directory count than some competitors, pricing structure unclear across sources, actual listing time varies (some directories take months to approve).
**Best for**: Budget founders who want manual submissions with transparent progress tracking via Airtable.
