# Famesters Platform Guide

## Company background

Famesters was founded in 2017 by **Pavel Beinia** (also CEO & Founder of BuzzGuru, the influencer marketing analytics platform). **Nadia Bubennikova** (Chief Business Officer) is Co-founder. The agency has ~40 employees and is headquartered in Limassol, Cyprus.

Famesters operates as the agency arm of the BuzzGuru ecosystem — using BuzzGuru's proprietary analytics technology for data-driven influencer discovery, campaign planning, and performance measurement.

**Client portfolio**: 1000+ brands worldwide
**Notable clients**: Opera, Konami, PUBG, FxPro, 1xBET, Playbet.io, Kupikod
**ROI claim**: Up to 3x

## Services

### 1. Campaign strategy and planning
- Industry and audience research
- Competitor analysis in influencer marketing
- Media planning and budget allocation
- KPI definition and funnel design
- Content strategy and creative direction

### 2. Creator selection and vetting
- AI-powered discovery using BuzzGuru analytics platform
- Manual vetting for audience authenticity and brand alignment
- Creator-brand matching based on niche, audience demographics, engagement
- Multi-platform sourcing (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitch)

### 3. Campaign execution
- Full outreach and negotiation management
- Content coordination and review
- Publication scheduling and cross-platform launch timing
- Real-time campaign monitoring

### 4. Measurement and reporting
- Unique tracking links and promo codes per creator
- UTM parameters for analytics integration
- Performance tracking: installs, registrations, LTV, ROI
- Post-campaign analysis with optimization recommendations
- Custom reporting cadence and format

## Pricing

Famesters uses custom, project-based pricing. No public rate card.

| Model | How it typically works |
|---|---|
| **Project-based** | Flat fee per campaign based on scope, creator count, and content volume |
| **Monthly retainer** | Ongoing partnership with dedicated team, multiple campaigns per month |
| **Performance-based elements** | Some campaigns include performance bonuses tied to KPIs (installs, registrations) |

Budget expectations (estimated from industry context):
- Single campaign: $10K-50K+ depending on creator tier and content volume
- Multi-market campaigns: $50K-200K+
- Enterprise retainers: $200K+/year

*No confirmed minimums — contact Famesters directly for scoping.*
