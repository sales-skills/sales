# FansRevenue Platform Reference

## Platform overview

FansRevenue is a fanbase monetization platform for adult content creators, cam models, and influencers. Powered by CrakRevenue (a 14+ year CPA network), it combines influencer and affiliate marketing to connect creators with exclusive brand partnerships. Creators earn commissions by promoting brands, other creators, and their own content through affiliate links, custom landing pages, and link-in-bio tools. Over 500 creators have earned more than $1 million in total commissions through the platform.

## Key modules

### Creator Hub
- Free profile claiming and personal branding
- Performance metrics and audience demographics dashboard
- Centralized management for all brand partnerships and promotions
- Profile customization for creator identity and content niche

### Brand Partnerships
14+ exclusive brand partnerships in the adult vertical:
- **Cam platforms**: Jerkmate, Streamate, MyFreeCams, Chaturbate, StripChat, CamSoda, LiveJasmin, BongaCams

### Creator-to-Creator Promotion
- Earn by promoting top industry creators to your audience
- Commission: 15-35% or $30 fixed per sale (varies by creator/offer)
- Cross-promotion builds network effects within the creator community

### Creator Academy

## Pricing and limits

- **Free to join** — no subscription or setup fees
- Commission-based model — creators earn a percentage of conversions they drive
- Commission rates: 15-40% depending on the offer/brand
- **CrakRevenue payout minimum**: $100 (earnings below threshold roll over)
- **Payment methods**: Wire transfers, PayPal, Paxum, Bitcoin, checks
- No limit on number of offers promoted simultaneously
- No limit on referral earnings (5% lifetime on referred creators)

## Integrations

- **CrakRevenue** (parent network) — powers all tracking, attribution, and payouts
- **DivaTraffic** (acquired) — traffic generation service for creator pages
- **OnlyNews** — featured placement for creator exposure
- **OneSignal** — push notification infrastructure
- **BrandItScan** — brand/content protection
- **Pineapple Support** — mental health resources
- No Zapier, webhook, or third-party API integrations documented
- No CRM integration — all management through FansRevenue dashboard

## Data model (no public API)

FansRevenue does not expose a public API. All interaction happens through the web dashboard:
- **Offers** — brand partnerships with specific terms, commission rates, and tracking links
- **Conversions** — tracked via CrakRevenue's attribution system (cookie-based)
- **Referrals** — creator-to-creator recruitment tracked by unique referral links
- **Earnings** — commission accrual with minimum payout thresholds

## Workflow setup

### Getting started as a creator
1. Visit fansrevenue.com and claim your free Creator Hub profile
2. Complete profile — add your content niche, social links, and bio
3. Browse available brand partnerships in the dashboard
4. Select offers that match your audience (cam platform fans, subscription content fans)
5. Generate your unique tracking links for each offer
6. Set up MyEroLink page with prioritized offers
7. Start promoting via your content channels (cam profile, social bio, direct messages)

### Optimizing promotion
1. Start with 2-3 offers that match your existing audience
2. Place highest-converting offers first on MyEroLink
3. Use custom landing pages for specific promotions
4. Track conversion rates per offer in the dashboard
5. Rotate underperforming offers out, scale winners
6. Use Creator Academy resources to improve promotional techniques
7. Consider creator-to-creator promotion for passive cross-promotion income

### Maximizing referral income
1. Share your referral link with creator friends and colleagues
2. Explain the platform's free model and commission potential
3. Each referred creator earns you 5% of their lifetime commissions
4. Focus on recruiting active, high-earning creators for maximum passive income
5. Use authentic outreach — spam damages relationships and reputation

## Deep dives

### CrakRevenue network relationship
FansRevenue is powered by CrakRevenue, one of the largest adult CPA networks (14+ years, 15,000+ active affiliates). Key implications:
- **Tracking**: All affiliate tracking and attribution runs through CrakRevenue's infrastructure
- **Payouts**: Processed through CrakRevenue — same $100 minimum, same payment methods
- **Offers**: FansRevenue curates a subset of CrakRevenue's 700+ offers specifically for creators
- **Support**: CrakRevenue's affiliate management team backs FansRevenue support
- **Compliance**: CrakRevenue handles legal, fiscal, and DMCA considerations

### Commission structure deep dive
- **Brand partnerships**: Typically 15-40% lifetime recurring commissions on referred user spend
- **Creator promotion**: 15-35% or $30 fixed per sale, varies by promoted creator
- **Referral program**: 5% lifetime on all earnings of referred creators, no cap
- **Cookie duration**: Typically 30 days (varies by offer — always confirm)
- **Payment schedule**: Monthly, once $100 threshold is met

### Content promotion best practices for adult creators
- **Cam profiles**: Add affiliate links to your profile bio and tip menu messages
- **OnlyFans/subscription platforms**: Use pinned posts and mass messages with tracking links
- **Social media**: MyEroLink as bio link (avoids direct adult links that trigger platform bans)
- **Direct messaging**: Personalized recommendations to engaged fans
- **Avoid**: Spamming links without context, promoting offers that don't match your audience
