## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about features and pricing that may change.*

- **Free tier requires a badge on your site.** Unlike Uneed (free with no badge) or ProductBurst (instant free), Fazier's free tier requires you to add a verification badge to your homepage or footer. If you don't want a badge, the Lite tier ($19) removes this requirement.
- **Traffic is very modest.** ~658 estimated monthly visits — this is primarily an SEO backlink play, not a traffic source. Don't expect significant referral visits.
- **15-day review for free submissions.** Manual moderation means your listing isn't instant. Plan accordingly if timing matters.
- **DR81 is from LaunchDirectories — verify independently.** Domain ratings fluctuate and sources can disagree. The site claims "DR 81+" for its guaranteed backlink tier. Verify with Ahrefs or similar after your listing goes live.
- **Lite tier backlink is conditional.** "High-authority backlink for top 3 daily products" — not guaranteed for all Lite submissions. Only Premium ($39) guarantees the backlink.
- **Pricing shows crossed-out "original" prices.** Lite was "$29" now "$19", Premium was "$59" now "$39", Super was "$79" now "$99" (wait — Super went UP?). Pricing display may be marketing-driven. Focus on current prices.
- **Small community means less voting activity.** Daily launches may have fewer votes and less engagement than larger platforms. This can be an advantage (less competition) or disadvantage (less social proof).

## Troubleshooting

### Free submission not approved after 15 days
**Symptom**: Submitted for free over 15 days ago, still not listed
**Cause**: Manual moderation queue may be backed up, or the badge wasn't detected on your site.
**Solution**: Verify the Fazier badge is visible on your homepage or footer. Check your email for any communication from Fazier. If still pending, consider upgrading to Lite ($19) for immediate publishing, or reach out via @FalakDigital on X/Twitter.

### Badge requirement feels intrusive
**Symptom**: Don't want to add a Fazier badge to your site but want the free listing
**Cause**: Free tier requires badge verification to ensure submissions are genuine.
**Solution**: The Lite tier ($19) removes the badge requirement and publishes immediately. If $19 isn't in budget, add the badge in the footer where it's less prominent — it only needs to be present, not dominant. You can also wait for a sale or promotion.

### Backlink not showing in Ahrefs after listing goes live
**Symptom**: Product listed on Fazier but backlink not appearing in SEO tools
**Cause**: New backlinks take time for crawlers to discover. Despite DR81, Fazier's low traffic means crawlers may visit less frequently.
**Solution**: Wait 2-6 weeks for crawlers to discover the link. Manually verify by inspecting your product page on fazier.com for a dofollow `<a>` tag. If the link is nofollow, check whether your tier includes a dofollow guarantee — only Premium ($39) guarantees it explicitly.
