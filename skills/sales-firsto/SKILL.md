## Step 3 — Firsto platform reference

### How Firsto works

Firsto is a product launch platform that emphasizes sustained discovery over launch-day spikes. Products become permanent, SEO-optimized pages that rank on Google for months — not just on launch day. Originally built by forking Open Launch, Firsto has diverged with its own pricing model, editorial reviews, and alternatives pages.

**Key difference**: Long-term SEO focus — product pages rank for brand keywords and appear on alternatives pages, generating ongoing organic traffic.

#### Launch flow
1. Create an account on Firsto
2. Submit your product: name, description, URL, screenshots
3. Choose a tier: Free (queue), Premium ($19.90), Pro ($59.90), or SEO Growth ($149)
4. All launches happen at **8:00 UTC** daily
5. Products appear in daily rankings, category browsing, and search

#### Pricing tiers

| Tier | Cost | Backlink | Queue | What you get |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | Dofollow for Top 3 only (badge required) | ~180 days, 5 slots/day | Permanent product page |
| Premium | $19.90/launch | Guaranteed DR59+ dofollow | 24 hours | Direct site link on all product cards |
| Pro | $59.90/launch | Guaranteed DR59+ dofollow | 24 hours | 30-day homepage feature (~3K impressions), mini review (50-80 words), star ratings in Google |
| SEO Growth | $149/launch | Guaranteed DR59+ dofollow + SEO article | 24 hours + 7-day article delivery | Custom 800-1200 word "[Product] review" article |
| Private Review | $249/report | N/A | ~7 days | Confidential UI/UX audit (P0-P3 severity), PDF report |

#### Free backlink conditions (important!)

The free tier backlink is **not automatic**. You must meet BOTH conditions:
1. **Finish in Top 3 daily ranking** — your product must be one of the 3 most visible products that day
2. **Display the Firsto badge** on your website — they verify this

If you don't meet both conditions, you get the listing but NOT the dofollow backlink. This is the same model as Open Launch.

### Firsto vs Open Launch

Firsto was forked from Open Launch, so the models are very similar. Key differences:

| Aspect | Firsto | Open Launch |
|---|---|---|
| Premium price | $19.90 | $12 |
| SEO article price | $149 | $59 |
| Free queue | ~180 days | Variable (shorter) |
| Claimed DR | 57-59+ | DR65 |
| Mini reviews | Yes (Pro tier, 50-80 words) | No |
| Homepage feature | 30 days (Pro tier) | None |
| Star ratings in Google | Yes (Pro tier) | No |
| Private UI/UX review | Yes ($249) | No |
| Traffic | 15K monthly visitors | Modest |

**Bottom line**: Open Launch is cheaper for the basic backlink ($12 vs $19.90) and has higher DR (65 vs 57). Firsto offers more premium add-ons (mini reviews, homepage features, private audits) and emphasizes long-term SEO via permanent product pages and alternatives pages.

| 6. Share externally | Post your Firsto listing on Twitter, communities | External traffic drives rankings |

### Which tier to choose

**Free ($0) when:**
- Budget is zero AND you can wait ~180 days
- You're confident you can reach Top 3 daily
- You're willing to display the Firsto badge on your site

**Premium ($19.90) when:**
- You want the DR59+ backlink guaranteed — no conditions
- You need to launch within 24 hours
- You want direct site links on all product cards

**Pro ($59.90) when:**
- You want 30-day homepage featured placement (~3K impressions)
- A mini editorial review adds credibility
- Star ratings in Google search results matter for your brand

**SEO Growth ($149) when:**
- You want a dedicated "[Product] review" article (800-1200 words)
- Long-term Google ranking for "[Your Product] review" keywords
- You're investing seriously in SEO and want content assets

**Private Review ($249) when:**
- You want honest UI/UX feedback before a major launch
- Bug findings prioritized by severity (P0-P3)
- Confidential — not published publicly

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and integration gotchas that may be outdated.*

- **Free backlink is NOT automatic.** Same model as Open Launch — only Top 3 daily get a dofollow backlink, and you must display the Firsto badge. Regular listings get nofollow links.
- **~180 day free queue is extremely long.** This is the longest free queue of any launch directory. If timing matters at all, the $19.90 Premium is your only option.
- **DR varies wildly by source.** LaunchDirectories reports DR57, other sources report DR29. The platform claims "DR59+ backlinks." Don't rely on exact numbers — verify current metrics before purchasing.
- **Forked from Open Launch but more expensive.** Premium is $19.90 vs Open Launch's $12. SEO article is $149 vs $59. Open Launch also has higher confirmed DR (65). Evaluate whether Firsto's extras (mini reviews, homepage features, alternatives pages) justify the price difference.
- **Small platform.** 15K monthly visitors and 2K+ projects is modest. Don't expect significant direct traffic — the value is SEO and the permanent product page.
- **No public API.** Everything is manual through the web interface.
- **5 free slots per day.** Combined with the ~180 day queue, getting a free launch slot is very competitive.

## Troubleshooting

### Stuck in the ~180 day free queue
**Symptom**: Submitted for free weeks ago, product hasn't launched
**Cause**: Firsto limits free launches to 5 slots/day. With a ~180 day queue, free submissions wait approximately 6 months.
**Solution**: Upgrade to Premium ($19.90) for 24-hour launch. If budget is tight, consider Open Launch's $12 Premium instead — it's cheaper and has higher DR. If you're committed to free, use the wait time to polish your listing and prepare other directory submissions.

### Free listing but no dofollow backlink
**Symptom**: Product is listed on Firsto but backlink is nofollow
**Cause**: Free-tier dofollow backlinks require BOTH conditions: (1) finishing Top 3 in daily ranking, and (2) displaying the Firsto badge on your website. Default free listings get nofollow links.
**Solution**: If the backlink matters, upgrade to Premium ($19.90) for guaranteed DR59+ dofollow. Or submit to directories with automatic dofollow backlinks: ProductBurst (DR46), SoloPush (DR46-47), LaunchingNext (DR51).

### Unsure if Firsto's DR claims are accurate
**Symptom**: Different sources show different DR numbers (29, 57, 59+)
**Cause**: DR changes over time and varies by measurement tool. Firsto is a newer platform whose authority is still growing.
**Solution**: Use Ahrefs' free Website Authority Checker to check current DR before purchasing. The paid tier guarantees "DR59+ backlinks" but verify independently. For a confirmed DR65 backlink, Open Launch Premium ($12) is a safer bet.
