## Step 4 — Actionable guidance

### When to submit to Futurepedia

**Submit if:**
- Your product is AI-powered (Futurepedia is AI-only)
- You want high-traffic directory exposure (~2M monthly visits)
- You can invest $497 for the Verified listing
- You want access to the YouTube network for potential tutorial/review coverage
- You're targeting professionals and business users adopting AI

**Deprioritize if:**
- Your product is not AI-related (Futurepedia only lists AI tools)
- Your budget is under $247 (no free tier available)
- You're looking for a quick, low-cost directory submission (many alternatives are free or under $50)
- Your primary goal is pure SEO backlinks (cheaper dofollow options exist via Fazier DR81 at $39, Uneed DR74 at $29.99)

### Optimizing your listing

1. **Category selection** — choose the most specific category matching your tool. Futurepedia's "Best of" lists within categories get additional editorial visibility.
2. **Product video** — Verified listings support video upload. A 60-90 second demo significantly increases engagement. The video may also be placed on the Tutorials page.
3. **Description** — lead with the problem your tool solves, not feature lists. Futurepedia's audience is professionals evaluating tools for business use.
4. **Tutorial content** — if you have a tutorial for your tool, submit it. Tutorials get placement on Futurepedia's dedicated Tutorials page with a link back to your listing.
5. **Timing** — coordinate your Futurepedia listing with other directory submissions. Use Futurepedia as a Phase 2 (paid fast-track) submission since it has a 2-day turnaround.

### Submission sequencing

In a multi-directory launch, Futurepedia fits in **Phase 2 (paid fast-track)** — its 2-business-day turnaround for Verified listings lets you coordinate timing with other paid fast-track submissions.

**Recommended sequence:**
1. Phase 1 — instant free listings (SoloPush, ProductBurst, DevHunt)
2. **Phase 2 — Futurepedia Verified ($497)** alongside other paid listings (Fazier $39, Open Launch $12, Uneed $29.99)
3. Phase 3 — free editorial queues (CtrlAlt.cc, MicroLaunch, StartupBase)
4. Phase 4 — Product Hunt main launch

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and integration gotchas that may be outdated.*

- **No free tier.** Unlike most directories (Altern, Uneed, Fazier all offer free submissions), Futurepedia requires payment. The Basic tier ($247) is sold out — the only current option is Verified ($497) or Enterprise (custom).
- **$497 is premium pricing.** Most directory paid tiers are $12-149. Futurepedia's pricing reflects its traffic (~2M/mo) but is 3-10x more expensive than alternatives. Compare: Fazier Premium $39 (DR81 dofollow), Open Launch $12 (DR65 dofollow), Uneed $29.99 (DR74 dofollow).
- **No public API.** There's no way to programmatically manage your listing or pull analytics.
- **Editorial rejection possible.** Submissions are reviewed and non-AI products may be rejected. Refund is provided if rejected, but only before publication.
- **"Sold out" tiers may return.** The Basic tier being sold out may be temporary or a scarcity tactic. Check the submit page for current availability.
- **Traffic estimates vary.** The ~2M/mo figure comes from multiple sources but actual referral traffic to your listing will be a fraction of total site visits.
- **YouTube coverage is not guaranteed.** Enterprise packages include YouTube sponsorship options, but Verified listings don't guarantee video coverage on the YouTube network.
- **Education platform is separate.** Futurepedia's 29 courses and AI Boot Camp are a separate offering — your tool listing doesn't automatically get featured in educational content.

## Troubleshooting

### Submitted and paid but listing not appearing
**Symptom**: Paid for Verified listing but tool isn't visible after 2+ business days
**Cause**: Editorial review may take longer during high-volume periods. The 2-business-day estimate is for Verified tier but actual turnaround may vary.
**Solution**: Email contact@futurepedia.io with your submission details and payment confirmation. If rejected, you should receive a full refund automatically. Check spam folder for any rejection notification.

### Unsure if $497 Verified is worth it
**Symptom**: Want Futurepedia exposure but $497 feels expensive
**Cause**: Futurepedia is 3-10x more expensive than most directory paid tiers. The value depends on your product's fit with their audience.
**Solution**: Start with cheaper directories first — Fazier ($39, DR81 dofollow), Open Launch ($12, DR65 dofollow), Uneed ($29.99, DR74 dofollow). These provide immediate backlink value at lower cost. Submit to Futurepedia once you've validated that directory traffic converts for your product. Use `/sales-launch-directory` to plan the full sequence.

### Want free listing but none available
**Symptom**: Looking for a free way to get listed on Futurepedia
**Cause**: Futurepedia discontinued free/complimentary listings. The cheapest option is $247 (Basic, currently sold out) or $497 (Verified).
**Solution**: Submit to free AI directories instead: Altern (free, dofollow backlinks, AI-only), PitchWall (free tier with 30+ day queue), and general startup directories with free tiers (Fazier free with badge, Uneed free queue, Open Launch free queue). Use `/sales-launch-directory` for the complete free directory list.
