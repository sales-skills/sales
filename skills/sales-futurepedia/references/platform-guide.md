## Verified badge program

The Verified checkmark indicates editorial review and approval. Benefits:
- Credibility signal — users trust verified tools more
- Enhanced listing page with more features than standard
- Potential featuring on the Deals page
- Inclusion in the personalized recommendation engine
- Video upload capability for product demos
- Tutorial placement on the Tutorials page (if you provide a tutorial)

For larger budgets, Futurepedia offers:

- **Creator/influencer campaigns** — leveraging their network of AI content creators

Contact contact@futurepedia.io for enterprise pricing.
