# GetMoreBacklinks Platform Reference

## Platform overview

GetMoreBacklinks is a managed directory submission service founded by Krissmann Gupta. It targets SaaS founders, solopreneurs, and startups who want to build backlinks through directory listings without doing the manual work themselves. The service handles directory selection, listing creation, and submission across 200+ directories.

Unlike DIY directory tools or databases (LaunchDirectories, StartupSubmit), GetMoreBacklinks is a done-for-you service — you provide your site details and they handle everything.

## When to use GetMoreBacklinks vs DIY

**Use GetMoreBacklinks when:**
- You're launching and want bulk directory coverage fast
- You don't have time to research and submit to 100+ directories individually
- You want someone else to handle the tedious submission process
- Your primary goal is broad backlink coverage, not optimizing each listing

**DIY with `/sales-launch-directory` when:**
- You want full control over which directories and listing quality
- You're targeting specific high-DR directories (Product Hunt, BetaList, Indie Hackers)
- You want to optimize each listing individually (descriptions, screenshots, timing)
- Budget is under $100 and you have time

**Hybrid approach (recommended for most startups):**
1. Manually submit to top 10-15 high-DR directories using `/sales-launch-directory` — these are the ones where listing quality matters most
2. Use GetMoreBacklinks Advanced ($127) for the long tail of 100 mid/low-tier directories — volume play where individual listing quality matters less
3. Track results with `/sales-semrush` — monitor new referring domains, DR changes, and organic traffic
