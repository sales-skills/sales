# Heepsy Platform Guide

## Influencer search and discovery

Heepsy's core feature is searching 11M+ influencer profiles across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Twitch.

**Search filters:**
- **Platform**: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitch
- **Category/Niche**: Beauty, fitness, tech, food, fashion, travel, gaming, etc.
- **Location**: Country, city, or region (creator location)
- **Followers**: Min/max range — micro (10K-100K), mid (100K-500K), macro (500K+)
- **Engagement rate**: Filter by minimum engagement percentage
- **Audience demographics**: Age, gender, location of the creator's followers (Plus plan+)
- **Authenticity**: Filter by audience quality score (Plus plan+)
- **Keywords/Hashtags**: Search by content keywords or hashtag usage
- **Lookalike**: Find creators similar to ones you already like

**Creator Detection**: Upload your existing customer list or follower list — Heepsy identifies which of your existing customers/followers are influencers.

**Marketplace**: Publish campaign briefs (gifting, paid, affiliate, UGC) and let creators apply to you instead of cold outreach. Filter applicants by country and platform.

## Analytics and authenticity

Each influencer profile includes an analytics report (consumes 1 credit per view):

- **Engagement rate**: Average across recent posts
- **Follower growth**: Trend over time — look for steady organic growth vs suspicious spikes
- **Audience demographics**: Age, gender, location, language breakdown
- **Audience quality score**: 1-100 scale assessing follower authenticity
- **Branded content frequency**: How often the creator posts sponsored content
- **Posting frequency**: How active the creator is

## Campaign management

**Campaign types:**
- **Gifting**: Send free products in exchange for content
- **Paid**: Fixed-fee collaborations with specific deliverables
- **Affiliate**: Commission-based partnerships tracked through links/codes
- **UGC**: User-generated content for brand use (ads, product pages)

**Marketplace workflow:**
1. Create a campaign brief with requirements, deliverables, and compensation
2. Publish to the Heepsy marketplace
3. Creators browse and apply to your campaign
4. Review applicants — check analytics, content style, audience fit
5. Accept creators and begin collaboration
6. Track content delivery in the media gallery

## Influencer CRM

Organize your influencer relationships:

- **Boards**: Group creators by campaign, niche, status, or any custom grouping
- **Pipeline stages**: Track each creator's status (contacted, negotiating, active, completed)
- **Conversations**: Message creators directly within Heepsy
- **Notes**: Add internal notes to each creator profile
- **Team sharing**: Multiple team members can access shared lists and boards

## Media gallery

Centralize all influencer-generated content:

- Content is collected as creators post
- Real-time notifications when new content is published
- Track performance metrics per content piece
- Download content for repurposing (with creator permission)

## Shopify and WooCommerce integration (Advanced plan)

**Available on Advanced ($269/mo) and Enterprise plans only.**

- Create campaign offers directly from your Shopify product catalog
- Generate unique affiliate links or promo codes per creator
- Track clicks and sales referred by each influencer
- Real-time sales attribution dashboard
- Automate gifting with product selection and shipping

**Setup:**
1. Go to Heepsy Settings -> Integrations
2. Connect your Shopify store (authorize the app)
3. Select products for campaigns
4. Assign tracking links/codes to creators
5. Monitor sales attribution in the campaign dashboard

## Data export and bulk outreach

- Export influencer lists as CSV or XLS
- Includes contact information (Plus plan+ for email addresses)
- Use exported data in external email tools or CRMs
- Heepsy does not have built-in email sending — export and use your preferred outreach tool

## Credit system

Heepsy uses a credit-based system where viewing an influencer's analytics report costs 1 credit.

- Credits reset monthly on your billing date
- Viewing the same profile multiple times within a billing period does not consume additional credits
- Search results and basic profile info (name, platform, follower count) are free to browse
- Credits are consumed only when you click into a detailed analytics report

**Credit conservation tips:**
- Use search filters aggressively before clicking into profiles — narrow your list with free filters first
- Check the basic profile preview (engagement, followers) before spending a credit on full analytics
- Build shortlists in search results view, then only open analytics for your top candidates
- Revisiting a profile you've already viewed that month is free
