## Step 3 — Huzzler platform reference

### How Huzzler works

Huzzler is a founder community and product launch platform built around a weekly competition model. Unlike Product Hunt's 24-hour window or ProductBurst's daily rankings, Huzzler runs a structured weekly Launch Arena where products compete for Gold, Silver, and Bronze placement.

**Key difference**: Community-first — Huzzler is an AI-free, guru-free founder community with Stripe-verified revenue. The launch competition is one feature of a broader community platform.

#### Launch Arena — weekly competition

| Phase | When | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| Submission | Monday – Wednesday | Submit your product to compete |
| Voting | Wednesday – Sunday | Community votes on submissions |
| Winners | Sunday | Gold, Silver, Bronze announced |
| Pin period | Following week | Winners pinned for 7 days at top of platform |

**What winners get:**
- **Gold/Silver/Bronze badges** — embeddable SVGs for your website
- **7-day pin** — product pinned at top of platform for a week
- **Dofollow backlink** — SEO backlink (winners only, not all submissions)

## Step 4 — Actionable guidance

### Launch Arena competition checklist

| Step | What to do | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Submit Mon-Wed | Enter the Launch Arena before Wednesday cutoff | Late submissions get fewer voting days |
| 2. Prepare visuals | Logo, screenshots, compelling description | First impression drives votes |
| 3. Tell your story | Why you built this, the problem it solves | Huzzler community values founder stories |
| 4. Engage in community | Post in #feedback, comment on others' products | Active members get reciprocal support |
| 5. Share externally | Post your Launch Arena entry on Twitter, communities | External traffic drives votes |
| 6. Respond to feedback | Reply to every comment and question | Shows you're a real, engaged founder |

### Winning Launch Arena

The weekly competition format means:
- **Smaller field**: ~18 contestants per week (much less competition than Product Hunt)
- **Multi-day voting**: Wed-Sun gives you 4+ days to gather votes (vs PH's 24 hours)
- **Reciprocity matters**: Engaging with other founders' products builds goodwill and return votes
- **Timing**: Submit early in the Mon-Wed window to maximize visibility during submission phase

### When Huzzler Black is worth it

**Buy Huzzler Black ($79) when:**
- You're launching multiple products and want lifetime visibility for all of them
- The verified badge adds credibility to your profile
- You want to remove ads from your experience
- You're betting on upcoming features (alternatives pages, problem directory)

**Skip Huzzler Black when:**
- You only need a single Launch Arena entry (free)
- The backlink is your primary goal (only winners get it regardless of Black status)
- Budget is tight — $79 is more than Open Launch Premium ($12) or CtrlAlt.cc ($39)

### Multi-platform launch strategy

Huzzler works best as one platform in a broader launch:

1. **SoloPush + ProductBurst** — instant listings (Day 1)
2. **Huzzler Launch Arena** — submit Monday for weekly competition
3. **Open Launch Premium ($12)** — next-day launch with DR65 backlink
4. **MicroLaunch** — 30-day leaderboard cycle
5. **CtrlAlt.cc + LaunchingNext** — curated directories (queue or pay to skip)
6. **Product Hunt** — save for main launch event

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and integration gotchas that may be outdated.*

- **Backlink is NOT automatic.** This is the most important gotcha. Unlike ProductBurst or SoloPush where every listing gets a dofollow backlink, Huzzler only gives dofollow backlinks to Launch Arena winners (Gold/Silver/Bronze). Regular product listings do not get an external backlink by default.
- **DR varies by source.** LaunchDirectories lists DR 41 in one place and DR 47 in another. The difference is not huge but don't rely on exact numbers.
- **Small community = small traffic.** 3,382 members and ~2.4K MAU reported. Don't expect significant direct traffic — value is community feedback and potential backlink.
- **Weekly competition resets.** If you don't win, your product doesn't get pinned or get the backlink. You can resubmit but competing repeatedly may feel spammy.
- **Huzzler Black features are mostly "coming soon."** The $79 buys lifetime visibility and a badge now, but the "alternatives to" pages and problem/solution directory are not yet live.
- **AI-free policy is strict.** Don't use AI-generated descriptions or spam. The community actively moderates for AI content.
- **No public API.** Everything is manual through the web interface.
- **Advertising uses a credit system.** Pricing adjusts based on platform impressions — $34 promoted product, but credit costs may change.

## Troubleshooting

### Submitted to Launch Arena but didn't win
**Symptom**: Product competed but didn't place Gold, Silver, or Bronze
**Cause**: Other products received more community votes during the Wed-Sun voting window. With ~18 contestants per week, only top 3 win.
**Solution**: Analyze what winners did differently — usually they promoted more actively on external channels and engaged more with the community during voting. Consider resubmitting in a future week with better promotion strategy. Meanwhile, your product listing still exists on Huzzler for community visibility — you just don't get the pin or backlink.

### Product listed but not getting community engagement
**Symptom**: Product is on Huzzler but barely getting feedback or views
**Cause**: Huzzler is community-driven — passive listings get minimal organic discovery. The platform has ~3,382 members total.
**Solution**: Engage actively in community categories. Post in #feedback with specific questions. Comment on other founders' products. Share wins in #wins (with Stripe-verified MRR if possible). The community rewards active participation. Also post in #showcases to get your product in front of more members.

### Unsure if Huzzler Black is worth $79
**Symptom**: Considering the premium tier but unsure about ROI
**Cause**: Most premium features (alternatives pages, problem directory) are "coming soon" — current value is verified badge, no ads, and lifetime visibility.
**Solution**: Buy if you're launching multiple products and want ongoing premium treatment. Skip if you're testing with one product — the free Launch Arena entry gives you the core competition experience. Compare: Open Launch Premium is $12 for a guaranteed DR65 backlink, CtrlAlt.cc is $39 for a week-long feature. Huzzler Black's $79 is a bet on the platform's growth and upcoming features.
