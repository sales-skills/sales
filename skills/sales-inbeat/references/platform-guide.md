# inBeat Platform Guide

## Company background

inBeat was co-founded by **David Morneau** (CEO) and is headquartered in Montreal, with offices in New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago. The company started as an influencer discovery SaaS platform (Instagram and TikTok database) and transitioned to a full-service creative growth agency plus free marketing toolkit.

**Important**: The paid influencer database/SaaS product was **shut down in March 2026**. The free toolkit and agency services continue to operate normally.

**Notable clients**: 7-Eleven, Bumble, Hopper, Nielsen IQ, Miro, Airbnb, Pinterest, Nestle, Disney, New Balance

**Industries served**: Mobile apps, ecommerce/DTC, CPG, healthcare/wellness, B2B, SaaS, finance, travel, technology

**G2 reviews**: 584 reviews — rated as a top influencer marketing platform for small businesses (G2 Spring 2025 Grid Report)

## UGC platform

inBeat's UGC platform connects brands with vetted creators for direct-response content production:

- **Creator matching** — matched with top creators based on brand fit and performance track record
- **Content review** — review all content and request revisions directly in the platform
- **Usage rights** — unlimited copyright for paid ads and social media
- **Creator training** — creators go through a "Conversion Bootcamp" to improve direct-response creative quality

*Note: UGC platform signups now redirect to Showcase (showca.se) — possible rebrand or partner platform.*

## Agency vs in-house decision framework

| Factor | Agency (inBeat) | In-house team |
|---|---|---|
| **Scope** | Full-service (influencer + paid + creative + strategy) | Typically specialized in 1-2 areas |
| **Time to launch** | 2-4 weeks | 3-6 months (hiring + onboarding) |
| **Creator network** | Pre-vetted relationships | Build from scratch |
| **Multi-channel** | Meta, TikTok, Snapchat, Google in one vendor | May need separate specialists per channel |
| **Cost** | Campaign-based or retainer | $300K-$800K/yr for full team |
| **Control** | Less direct control over execution | Full creative control |
| **Scalability** | Easy to scale up/down by campaign | Fixed headcount |

**When to choose inBeat:**
- Need full-service across influencer + paid media + creative (not just one channel)
- DTC, CPG, mobile app, or healthcare brand wanting performance-focused creative
- Want UGC production at scale with unlimited usage rights
- Campaign budgets $20K+ with clear performance KPIs
- Need multi-platform paid media management (Meta + TikTok + Snapchat + Google)

**When to stay in-house or use self-serve:**
- Budget under $20K per campaign — use self-serve platforms (Modash $199/mo, Heepsy $49/mo, Hypefy pay-per-campaign)
- Only need influencer discovery, not full-service management
- Need daily community management (not campaign-burst model)
- Want direct access to a dashboard and analytics (inBeat is agency-delivered, not self-serve)

## Campaign workflow (typical)

1. **Briefing** — inBeat conducts market research, defines KPIs and target audience
2. **Strategy** — Creative direction, channel selection, content themes, creator profiles
3. **Creator selection** — Match from vetted creator network based on audience fit and performance
4. **Content production** — Creators produce content, brand reviews and approves in platform
5. **Paid amplification** — Best-performing content fueled through paid channels (Meta, TikTok, Snapchat, Google)
6. **Optimization** — A/B testing creative variants, audience optimization, budget reallocation
7. **Reporting** — Performance tracking against KPIs, insights for next campaign
