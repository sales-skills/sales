# influData Platform Guide

## Creator Discovery

influData's AI-powered search finds creators across 20M+ profiles on Instagram (15M), TikTok (10M), YouTube (5M+), Twitch (~100K), Snapchat, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

### Prompt-based AI search
- Type natural-language queries: "fitness influencers in Germany with 50K-200K followers who post about home workouts"
- AI interprets intent and returns ranked results
- Supports 15+ filter parameters: location, audience demographics, engagement rate, follower count, content category, language, brand collaborations

### Creator Lookalike Builder
- Select up to 3 brands or 10 individual influencers as seed profiles
- AI generates lists of creators matching those characteristics
- Useful for scaling a working creator roster or finding similar profiles in new markets

### Collections
- Save creators to collections (like playlists) for organizing campaigns
- Share collections across team members (All-In and Enterprise plans)
- Export as CSV or PDF

## Audience Analysis

50+ metrics per creator for vetting quality and fit.

### Demographics
- **Location** — audience geographic distribution (country, city)
- **Age and gender** — audience age brackets and gender split
- **Language** — primary languages spoken by audience
- **Interests** — what topics the audience engages with

### Quality signals
- **Fake follower detection** — identifies bot accounts and purchased followers
- **Engagement authenticity** — distinguishes real engagement from inflated metrics
- **Quality scores** — benchmarks creators against similar profiles in their category
- **Growth trends** — organic vs. suspicious follower growth patterns

### Audience overlap
- Compare 2+ creators to see shared audience percentage
- Essential for avoiding redundant reach when booking multiple creators for the same campaign
- Available on all plans

### Brand collaboration history
- See which brands a creator has worked with previously
- Identify potential conflicts (competitors) or alignment (complementary brands)
- Historical campaign data viewable on the creator profile

## Campaign Management

Track influencer campaigns in real time without manual follow-up.

### Content tracking
- **Automatic detection** of posts, stories, reels, and videos
- **Story archival** — captures ephemeral content before it disappears (24hr stories)
- Stories are auto-detected — no need for creators to send screenshots
- Track across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and more

### KPIs and reporting
- **EMV (Earned Media Value)** — estimated value of organic impressions
- **CPM (Cost per Mille)** — cost per 1,000 impressions
- **Reach** — unique accounts exposed to campaign content
- **Engagement** — likes, comments, shares, saves
- **Live dashboard** — real-time KPI updates as content goes live

### Sentiment analysis
- **Semantic comment analysis** — AI reads and categorizes comments (positive, negative, neutral)
- **Shitstorm detection** — alerts when a creator's content triggers controversial or negative reactions at scale
- **Campaign sentiment scoring** — aggregate sentiment across all campaign content

### Campaign seats
- Pro: 10 campaign seats
- All-In: 30 seats
- Enterprise: 60 seats

## Social Listening

Monitor brand mentions and discover UGC across platforms.

- **Brand mention tracking** — detect when your brand is mentioned in posts, stories, captions
- **Hashtag tracking** — follow branded and industry hashtags
- **UGC discovery** — find user-generated content about your products
- **Retrospective data** — up to 1 year of historical mention data
- **Tracker limits**: Pro (3), All-In (10), Enterprise (20)

## Outreach & Mailing

Send creator email campaigns directly from influData.

- **Email campaigns** — send personalized outreach to creators from the platform
- **Dynamic placeholders** — merge fields for creator name, handle, audience size, etc.
- **Templates** — save and reuse outreach templates
- **Tracking** — open rate, reply rate, bounce rate
- **Email validation** — built-in deliverability checks before sending
- **Built-in inbox** — manage creator replies without switching to your email client
- **Monthly limits**: Pro (20 emails/mo), All-In (100 emails/mo), Enterprise (500 emails/mo)

## Co-Pilot AI

AI chatbot available on creator profiles.

- **Profile summaries** — generates a plain-language overview of a creator's performance, audience, and brand fit
- **Q&A** — ask specific questions about a creator ("Is this creator's audience mostly in the US?", "How has their engagement changed in the last 6 months?")
- **Campaign recommendations** — suggests creators based on campaign goals

## Chrome Extension

View creator insights directly on Instagram and TikTok without leaving the platform.

- Install from Chrome Web Store
- Hover or click on a profile to see key metrics: follower count, engagement rate, audience demographics
- Quick-add creators to influData collections from the browser
- Available on all plans
