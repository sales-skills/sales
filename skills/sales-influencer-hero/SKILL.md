Influencer Hero is an all-in-one influencer marketing platform used by 1,200+ brands and 50+ agencies. Offices in New York, London, Dubai, and Singapore.

**Core modules:**

| Module | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Influencer Discovery** | Search 200M+ profiles across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and blogs. Filter by audience demographics, location, engagement, niche, follower range. AI-powered search with natural language queries. |
| **Automated Outreach** | AI-personalized email campaigns + Instagram/TikTok DM outreach. Templates, A/B testing, automated follow-ups. Gmail/Outlook/SMTP/IMAP integration. |
| **Influencer CRM** | Relationship management with deal pages, outreach boards, relationship boards. Track comms, status, performance, and payments per influencer. |

### Key workflows

#### Influencer discovery

1. **Keyword/AI search** — type natural language queries or use filters
2. **Apply filters** — audience demographics, location, engagement rate, follower range, niche, platform
3. **Review profiles** — engagement metrics, audience quality, recent content, brand collaborations
4. **Save to board** — add to outreach board or relationship board
5. **Use Chrome extension** — discover and save creators while browsing Instagram, TikTok, YouTube

#### Outreach campaign setup

1. **Connect email account** — Gmail, Outlook, or custom SMTP/IMAP
2. **Create campaign** — name, target audience, messaging
3. **Write templates** — use AI personalization to reference specific creator content
4. **Set follow-up sequence** — automated follow-ups with configurable delays
5. **A/B test** — test subject lines, message body, send times
6. **Launch** — monitor open rates, reply rates, and response quality

#### Affiliate and discount code tracking

1. **Generate unique codes** — per influencer, per campaign
2. **Distribute codes** — via outreach or deal pages
3. **Track usage** — real-time sales attribution per code
4. **Monitor for leakage** — code leakage prevention detects unauthorized sharing
5. **Process payouts** — commission calculation and payment management

#### Product gifting via Shopify

1. **Select products** — choose items to gift from your Shopify catalog
2. **Create gifting campaign** — set expectations, deliverables
3. **Send offers** — influencers accept and enter shipping info
4. **Auto-create orders** — orders flow into Shopify for fulfillment
5. **Track posting** — monitor which gifted creators post content

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and integration gotchas that may be outdated.*

- **Search filters can be inconsistent.** Users report that discovery filters sometimes don't apply correctly to results. If results look wrong, try narrowing your search with more specific filters or using the AI search with natural language instead of filter combinations.
- **Email accounts disconnect silently.** Gmail/Outlook connections can lose authentication without notification. Check your connected accounts regularly — reconnect if outreach stops sending. The platform doesn't always alert you when a connection drops.
- **Follow-up emails may start new threads.** Some users report follow-ups being sent as new email threads instead of replies to the original message. This makes tracking harder for both you and the influencer. Test your follow-up sequence with a test email address before launching.
- **Pricing is per brand.** If you manage multiple brands (agency model), each brand requires a separate subscription. This adds up quickly — evaluate the Business plan or contact sales for agency pricing.
- **No creator-side portal.** Unlike marketplace platforms (Afluencer, Collabstr), Influencer Hero doesn't have a portal where creators can sign up and browse brand opportunities. All outreach is brand-initiated. Onboarding a single known creator requires adding them manually or creating a campaign for them.
- **UGC capture and reporting are Pro+ only.** The Standard plan covers core modules (search, outreach, CRM, affiliate, gifting) but UGC post capture and reporting/analytics dashboards require the Pro plan ($1,049/mo) or higher.
- **Steep learning curve for small teams.** The platform is feature-rich, which means new users often feel overwhelmed. Start with one module (usually discovery + outreach), get comfortable, then expand to CRM, affiliate, and gifting.
- **14-day free trial may not be enough.** Given the learning curve, 14 days is tight to evaluate all modules. Focus your trial on the 1-2 features most critical to your decision.

## Troubleshooting

### Discovery filters returning irrelevant results
**Symptom**: Applied audience demographics and niche filters but results don't match criteria
**Cause**: Filter logic can be inconsistent, especially with multiple filters combined
**Solution**: Try fewer filters at once — start broad (platform + follower range) and narrow incrementally. Use the AI natural language search instead of filter combinations for better results. Alternatively, use the Chrome extension to find creators while browsing social media and save them directly.

### Outreach emails not being sent
**Symptom**: Campaign is active but emails aren't going out
**Cause**: Email account disconnected (Gmail/Outlook OAuth token expired) or monthly outreach limit reached
**Solution**: Check Settings → Connected Accounts to verify your email is still authenticated. Reconnect if needed. Check your plan's monthly outreach limit (Standard: 1K, Pro: 5K, Business: 10K) — if you've hit the limit, you'll need to wait for the next billing cycle or upgrade.

### Influencer posts not appearing in UGC library
**Symptom**: Influencers have posted about your brand but content isn't captured
**Cause**: UGC capture requires Pro plan or higher. Also, auto-capture depends on correct hashtag/mention tracking configuration.
**Solution**: Verify you're on Pro or Business plan. Check that you've configured the correct hashtags and @mentions for tracking. Note that capture may take up to 24 hours to detect new posts. For Instagram Stories, capture must happen within the 24-hour story window.
