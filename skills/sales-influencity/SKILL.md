## Step 3 — Influencity platform reference

### Discover

Search 200M+ creator profiles across Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.

**Key capabilities:**
- **Advanced filters** — interests, brand affinity, audience demographics, location, follower count, engagement rate
- **Audience analysis** — demographics, fake follower detection, audience overlap between creators
- **Lookalike search** — find creators similar to a known good performer
- **Monthly Results** — each profile viewed in search results consumes 1 credit

**Workflow:**
1. Define your ideal creator profile (niche, platform, follower range, audience demographics)
2. Run keyword or filter-based searches to build a candidate list
3. Analyze shortlisted profiles for audience quality and fake followers
4. Save promising creators to the IRM for outreach

### IRM (Influencer Relationship Management)

Personal CRM for managing influencer relationships.

**Key capabilities:**
- **Influencer database** — create lists, tag and organize creators, track negotiation history (pricing, interactions)
- **Profile analysis** — follower quality, audience demographics, engagement metrics across platforms
- **Audience overlap** — compare lists to check unique vs shared followers
- **Email and phone access** — contact info for associated profiles (availability varies)
- **Monthly Analyses** — each profile refresh in IRM consumes 1 analysis credit
- **Recruitment landing pages** — create branded application pages for inbound influencer interest

**Lists:**
- Group creators by campaign, tier, niche, or status
- Analyze combined audience demographics across a list
- Export lists for external use

### Campaigns

End-to-end campaign workflow management.

**Key capabilities:**
- **Workflow visualization** — Kanban-style boards with campaign stages
- **Task assignment** — assign deliverables with pricing, scheduling, and deadlines
- **Shopify integration** — select products from your Shopify store for gifting without leaving Influencity; generate gift codes for product redemption
- **Coupon tracking** — create and assign unique discount codes per creator
- **Bulk email outreach** — send campaign invitations and follow-ups at scale (1,200-4,000 emails/mo depending on plan)
- **Email integration** — connect Gmail or Outlook for personalized outreach from your own address

**Campaign setup checklist:**
1. Create campaign and define brief (objectives, deliverables, timeline)
2. Select creators from IRM lists
3. Set up Shopify gifting if sending products
4. Create unique coupon codes for tracking
5. Send outreach via bulk email or integrated email
6. Track acceptances and content submissions
7. Review deliverables against brief

### Reports

Publication performance analysis and campaign reporting.

**Key capabilities:**
- **URL-based tracking** — submit publication URLs to analyze performance
- **Metrics breakdown** — by social network, date, and individual influencer
- **Monthly Post Analyses** — each publication analyzed consumes 1 credit
- **Export options** — PDF, Excel, or shareable public links
- **Cross-campaign comparison** — compare performance across multiple campaigns

**Important:** Post tracking requires manual URL submission. Influencity does not auto-detect publications — you need the creator to share their post URLs or find them yourself.

### Social Hub

Multi-channel social media management.

**Key capabilities:**
- **Content calendar** — visual planning across multiple channels
- **Collaboration** — team-based content creation and editing
- **Approval workflows** — review and approve content before publishing
- **Multi-brand support** — manage content calendars for multiple brands (agency use case)

*Note: Social Hub is a distinct module from the influencer campaign tools. It manages your own social media channels, not influencer content.*

### Monitoring

Brand and competitor intelligence.

**Key capabilities:**
- **Brand tracking** — monitor mentions of your brand across 100M+ sources
- **Competitor analysis** — track competitor mentions and share of voice
- **Trend detection** — identify emerging topics and hashtags in your industry
- **Sentiment analysis** — gauge how people feel about your brand or campaigns

*Available on Business plan and above.*

### Influencer Payments

Multi-currency payment processing for creator compensation.

Credits reset monthly. Unused credits do not roll over.

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and integration gotchas that may be outdated.*

- **Credits run out faster than expected.** Users frequently report running out of Monthly Analyses before they finish vetting creators. Plan your discovery → analysis workflow carefully. Use Discover filters to narrow before spending analysis credits.
- **Post tracking is manual.** You must input post URLs yourself to track performance — Influencity does not auto-detect influencer publications. This creates administrative overhead for large campaigns. Establish a process for creators to submit their post URLs.
- **No Instagram Stories or YouTube Shorts auto-tracking.** Ephemeral content (Stories) and short-form content (Shorts) cannot be tracked automatically. Creators must share their own analytics for these formats.
- **Shopify only for ecommerce.** No native integration with WooCommerce, BigCommerce, or Magento. If you're not on Shopify, you'll need to manage gifting and tracking manually or via workarounds.
- **Social listening is Business+ only.** Monitoring module (brand tracking, competitor analysis, sentiment) is not available on the Professional plan.
- **API is not publicly documented.** Even on Business/Enterprise plans, API access requires contacting support. Don't assume you can build integrations quickly.
- **Maintenance windows reported.** Some users report occasional extended maintenance periods that interrupt workflow. If running time-sensitive campaigns, build in buffer time.
- **Pricing varies across sources.** Published pricing differs between review sites. Always verify current pricing directly at influencity.com/pricing.

- **Self-improving**: If you discover something not covered here, append it to `references/learnings.md` with today's date.

## Troubleshooting

### Running out of Monthly Analyses too fast
**Symptom**: Analysis credits depleted mid-month, can't vet more creators
**Cause**: Analyzing too many candidates in Discover before narrowing the list, or repeatedly refreshing the same profiles
**Solution**: Use Discover filters to narrow results before spending analysis credits. Save promising creators to IRM lists immediately. Only analyze shortlisted creators, not every search result. If consistently hitting limits, consider upgrading from Professional (150) to Business (500) or contact sales for credit add-ons.

### Post performance not updating in Reports
**Symptom**: Added a post URL to Reports but metrics seem stale or missing
**Cause**: Reports require manual URL submission and may take time to pull data. Ephemeral content (Stories) and Shorts are not supported for auto-tracking.
**Solution**: Verify the URL is correct and publicly accessible. Wait 24-48 hours for initial data pull. For Stories and Shorts, ask creators to share their native analytics screenshots instead. Each post analyzed costs 1 Monthly Post Analysis credit.

### Shopify gifting not syncing products
**Symptom**: Connected Shopify but products aren't appearing in Influencity campaigns
**Cause**: Integration requires copying and pasting the Shopify API token correctly. Products may take time to sync.
**Solution**: Go to Accounts & Settings → Integrations → Shopify. Verify the store URL and API token. Re-enter the API token if needed. Check that products are published and visible in your Shopify storefront. Contact Influencity support if sync doesn't complete within a few hours.
