## Step 3 — InstaJet.io platform reference

### Platform overview

InstaJet.io is an Instagram influencer marketplace owned by Mirafox Holding (which also operates Telega.io for Telegram ads). It connects brands with 10,000+ manually vetted Instagram influencers across 50+ countries for native ad placements in Instagram Stories.

**Key terminology:**
- **Placement** — a single ad slot in an influencer's Instagram Stories
- **Native recommendation** — ad in the influencer's own voice and style
- **Ad creative** — brand-provided ready-made ad content
- **Approval workflow** — brand reviews and approves content before it goes live

### Influencer discovery

InstaJet's catalog is openly browsable (no account required to view). Filter by:

| Filter | Options |
|---|---|
| **Topic/niche** | Lifestyle, beauty, fitness, food, travel, tech, fashion, etc. |
| **Geography** | 50+ countries, filterable by country |
| **Audience gender** | Male, female, mixed |
| **Audience age** | Age range targeting |
| **Account size** | Follower count ranges |
| **Cost-per-view** | Budget-based filtering |
| **Engagement rate** | Performance metrics visible per influencer |

**Influencer vetting**: Every influencer undergoes manual assessment for:
- Authenticity (no fake followers)
- Active audience engagement
- Content quality
- Minimum 10,000 followers required to join the platform

**Shopping cart model**: Add multiple influencers to your cart like an ecommerce store. This makes it easy to bundle influencers for a multi-creator campaign.

### Campaign setup workflow

1. **Browse and select influencers** — use filters to find creators matching your target audience
2. **Add to cart** — select influencers and add them to your campaign
3. **Choose ad format**:
   - **Native recommendation** — influencer creates content in their own voice
   - **Ready-made creative** — you provide the ad creative for the influencer to post
4. **Submit campaign** — provide campaign brief, links, and any creative assets
5. **Content review** — InstaJet's approval workflow lets you review content before it goes live
6. **Campaign confirmation** — within 24 hours
7. **Content goes live** — within 36 hours of confirmation
8. **Track results** — monitor via analytics dashboard

### Approval and content review

- Brand reviews all content before publication
- Approve, request changes, or reject placements
- Full control over messaging and brand safety
- Communication tools built into the platform for brand-influencer messaging

### Pricing and payment

**Plan tiers:**

| Plan | Price | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| Start | $550 | Testing InstaJet with a few placements |
| Standard | $920 | Small campaigns, single product launches |
| Expert | $2,500 | Regular campaigns, multiple influencers |
| Pro | $10,000 | Scaling campaigns, agency-level usage |
| Corporate | $50,000 | Enterprise, large-scale global campaigns |

**Payment flow (escrow model):**
- Funds held in escrow until content is published
- Payment released to influencer only after verified publication
- Supports bank transfers, cards, e-wallets, and cryptocurrency
- Multi-currency support across 50+ countries

**Managed service option**: InstaJet also offers personal management — their team handles influencer selection, strategic campaign planning, and execution for brands that want a hands-off approach.

## Gotchas

> *Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and integration gotchas that may be outdated.*

- **Instagram Stories only** — InstaJet focuses exclusively on Instagram Stories placements. No feed posts, Reels, TikTok, or YouTube. If you need multi-format campaigns, use a broader platform like Collabstr or Aspire.
- **No public API** — no programmatic access for campaign creation or reporting. Everything is done through the web UI.
- **No CRM or Zapier integration** — campaign data stays within InstaJet. You'll need to export reports manually.
- **Minimum 10K followers** — the influencer pool excludes nano-influencers (under 10K). If you need nano-influencer campaigns, try Afluencer or Collabstr.
- **Pricing is plan-based, not per-creator** — unlike Collabstr where you pay per creator, InstaJet uses tiered plans starting at $550. This can be expensive for testing with just one or two creators.
- **Cryptocurrency payments** — while supported, crypto payment processing may take longer than card payments.
- **Dubai-based entity** — InstaJet Technologies FZCO is registered in Dubai. Check if your procurement team has specific requirements for international vendor payments.

## Troubleshooting

**Campaign not getting confirmed within 24 hours**
- Check that your campaign brief is complete (product link, ad format, creative assets if applicable)
- Verify your payment has been processed
- Use the platform's built-in messaging to contact your assigned influencers
- Contact 24/7 support if the delay exceeds 48 hours

**Low engagement on Stories placements**
- Review the influencer's historical engagement rate before selecting (visible in the catalog)
- Prefer native recommendations over ready-made creatives — they typically get 2-3x higher engagement
- Check if your target audience matches the influencer's actual audience demographics
- Consider running multiple placements with the same influencer for frequency

**Can't find influencers in my niche/geography**
- InstaJet's database is 10K+ creators — smaller than platforms like Modash (350M+) or HypeAuditor (223M+)
- Try broadening your geography or niche filters
- If your niche is very specific, consider using a larger database platform for discovery and InstaJet for execution
- Contact InstaJet's managed service team — they may have off-platform influencer connections
