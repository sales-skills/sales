## Step 4 — Actionable guidance

### For users submitting (free tier)

1. Create account at justgotfound.com
2. Prepare: clear product name, problem-focused tagline, screenshots of product in action
3. Submit your product to the most relevant category
4. Add UTM tracking: `?utm_source=justgotfound&utm_medium=directory&utm_campaign=launch`
5. Engage with any votes or comments
6. Share your listing link to drive community engagement

**Note**: The dofollow backlink from DR23 has modest SEO value. For higher-impact backlinks, prioritize SoloPush (DR46), ProductBurst (DR46), Open Launch (DR65), or MicroLaunch (DR59).

### For users considering paid promotion

**Compare with alternatives:**
- **$60 on JustGotFound (1 week)** = featured placement on a DR23 platform
- **$12 on Open Launch** = guaranteed DR65 dofollow backlink + next-day launch (one-time)
- **$9.90 on OpenHunts** = guaranteed DR50 dofollow backlink + next-Monday launch
- **$39 on CtrlAlt.cc** = 1-week homepage feature + dofollow backlink (DA30)

**Verdict**: The paid promotion tiers are expensive relative to alternatives. Unless you specifically need visibility on JustGotFound, better backlink value per dollar exists at Open Launch ($12 for DR65) and OpenHunts ($9.90 for DR50).

JustGotFound fits into **Phase 1 — Instant listings** as a free, no-queue submission with a dofollow backlink. The DR23 is low compared to other instant-listing platforms (SoloPush DR46, ProductBurst DR46), but it's still a free dofollow backlink worth including in a broad directory sweep. ## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about pricing and features that may change.*

- **DR23 is low.** This is among the lowest DR in the directory landscape. The dofollow backlink has minimal SEO impact compared to DR46+ alternatives. Still worth submitting for free, but don't prioritize it over higher-DR options.
- **20% tax on all paid tiers.** The listed prices don't include the 20% tax JustGotFound charges to "cover payment processing fees and platform maintenance costs." A $50 promotion actually costs $60.
- **Very new platform.** Launched June 2025 with ~20 Product Hunt upvotes. Traffic data is unavailable. Set realistic expectations for referral traffic and community engagement.
- **No editorial curation.** Unlike curated directories (CtrlAlt.cc, LaunchingNext, Awesome Indie), JustGotFound has no editorial review. This means lower barrier to entry but also less quality signal for being listed.
- **Paid promotion is expensive relative to value.** $60/week for featured placement on a DR23 platform compares poorly to $12 for a guaranteed DR65 backlink on Open Launch.
- **Earnings program details unclear.** The platform mentions a content monetization/earnings program, but specifics on payouts and requirements are not documented.

## Troubleshooting

### Low engagement after submitting
**Symptom**: Product listed but getting very few votes or views
**Cause**: JustGotFound is a newer platform (launched June 2025) with modest traffic. Community engagement is still building.
**Solution**: Don't rely solely on organic discovery. Share your JustGotFound listing on your own channels to drive initial votes. Focus your engagement efforts on higher-traffic directories (Product Hunt, ProductBurst, SoloPush).

### Backlink not appearing in SEO tools
**Symptom**: Submitted and listed, but Ahrefs/Moz doesn't show the backlink
**Cause**: New platforms take time to be crawled. JustGotFound's low DR means search engines crawl it less frequently.
**Solution**: Wait 2-4 weeks for crawlers to pick it up. Verify manually by inspecting the HTML of your listing page for a dofollow link. If the link is present but not indexed, it will appear in SEO tools eventually. Focus on higher-DR directory backlinks for faster indexing.

### Not sure if paid promotion is worth it
**Symptom**: Considering paid tiers but unsure of ROI
**Cause**: JustGotFound's modest traffic and low DR make paid promotion a harder sell compared to alternatives.
**Solution**: Unless you have a specific reason to invest in JustGotFound visibility (e.g., strong category match, existing community presence), redirect your budget to Open Launch ($12 for DR65 backlink) or OpenHunts ($9.90 for DR50 backlink). These offer better backlink value per dollar.
