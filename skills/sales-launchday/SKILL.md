5. **Slack community access** — connect with other LaunchDay makers
6. **Applaud/voting system** — community engagement during the event
7. **Deal unlocks** — users share on social media to access your exclusive discount

### How LaunchDay compares to directories

LaunchDay is fundamentally different from traditional startup directories — it's an **event**, not a listing.

| Aspect | LaunchDay | Free directories (SoloPush, ProductBurst) | Paid directories (Open Launch $12) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cost | $99 (if selected) | Free | $12-$149 |
| Model | 24-hour event, 1-2/month | Always-on listing | Always-on listing |
| Backlink | Unknown | Confirmed dofollow | Guaranteed dofollow |
| Podcast | Yes — 30-min interview | No | No |
| Influencer promo | Yes — 100K+ followers | No | No |
| Discount required | >40% | No | No |
| VC-backed allowed | No | Yes | Yes |
| Permanent listing | Archive only | Full listing | Full listing |

### When LaunchDay makes sense

**Good fit:**
- You're bootstrapped and want exposure beyond just a backlink
- You value the podcast interview and influencer promotion more than the listing itself
- You can offer a meaningful discount (>40%) without hurting margins
- You're willing to invest $99 + time for a 30-min video interview

**Not ideal:**
- Your primary goal is SEO backlinks — unknown DR/dofollow status makes this unreliable for backlinks
- You're VC-backed — not eligible
- You can't offer >40% discount — required for participation
- You want a permanent, always-on directory listing — LaunchDay is a one-time event with archive placement

## Step 4 — Actionable guidance

### Preparing your LaunchDay submission

1. **Check eligibility**: Bootstrapped/indie? Can offer >40% discount? Willing to do video?
2. **Prepare your deal**: Create a specific discount code or landing page with >40% off
3. **Submit at itslaunchday.com/submit-product** — $99 fee is only charged if selected
4. **Prepare for the video interview**:
   - Have your product demo ready (working, not a prototype walkthrough)
   - Prepare your founder story — why you built it, what problem it solves
   - Keep it conversational — this becomes a podcast episode
5. **Add UTM tracking**: `?utm_source=launchday&utm_medium=directory&utm_campaign=launch`

### Maximizing your LaunchDay event

- **Promote the event yourself** — share on your social channels that you're featured
- **Engage during the 24-hour window** — respond to comments, thank people who applaud
- **Track deal unlocks** — monitor how many people unlock your discount
- **Follow up in Slack** — connect with other makers in the LaunchDay community

### Should you pay $99?

**Compare with alternatives:**
- **$99 on LaunchDay** = event featuring + podcast + influencer promo + Slack community + unknown backlink
- **$99 on LaunchingNext** = 1-business-day expedited review + DR51 dofollow backlink
- **$99 split across directories** = Open Launch Premium ($12) + CtrlAlt.cc ($39) + MicroLaunch Pro ($39) = 3 guaranteed dofollow backlinks + permanent listings

**If your goal is backlinks**: Split the budget across directories instead.
**If your goal is exposure + podcast + community**: LaunchDay offers unique value that directories don't.
**If budget allows both**: Do LaunchDay AND directory submissions.

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about pricing and event model that may change.*

- **Very early platform.** Only 4 events held as of early 2026. Track record is limited, and the platform's longevity is unproven.
- **Backlink value unknown.** No data on DR or dofollow/nofollow status. Don't count on LaunchDay for SEO value — treat any backlink as a bonus.
- **$99 is non-trivial for indie makers.** Compare with free directories (SoloPush, ProductBurst) and cheaper paid options (OpenHunts $9.90, Open Launch $12) before committing.
- **>40% discount required.** This can be a significant margin hit depending on your product. AI products with high inference costs get an exception, but SaaS products generally don't.
- **Video interview is mandatory.** If you're camera-shy or time-constrained, factor in the 30-minute commitment. The video becomes a public podcast episode.
- **Limited availability.** 1-2 events per month, max 20 products. Rejection is possible even after paying (payment is only processed if selected).
- **Event-based, not always-on.** Your featuring lasts 24 hours. After that, you're in the archive — not on the homepage.

- **Self-improving**: If you discover something not covered here, append it to `references/learnings.md` with today's date.

## Troubleshooting

### Not selected after submitting
**Symptom**: Submitted and paid $99 but wasn't selected for an event
**Cause**: LaunchDay curates strictly — only 20 products per event, and they prioritize diverse product categories
**Solution**: Your $99 is only charged if selected, so no financial loss. Reach out to @dagorenouf on Twitter for feedback on why you weren't selected. Consider submitting again for a future event. Meanwhile, submit to free directories (SoloPush, ProductBurst) for immediate listings.

### Not sure what discount to offer
**Symptom**: Required to offer >40% but unsure what's sustainable
**Cause**: Deep discounts can hurt margins, especially for early-stage products
**Solution**: Consider offering the discount on annual plans (higher LTV offsets the discount), limiting it to the first 50 users, or creating a special LaunchDay-only tier. If you have high infrastructure costs (AI inference, hosting), mention this in your submission — they make exceptions.

### Low engagement during the event
**Symptom**: Featured on LaunchDay but few applauds or deal unlocks
**Cause**: LaunchDay events depend on community size, which is still growing (4 events so far)
**Solution**: Actively promote the event on your own channels — share the LaunchDay link on Twitter, your email list, and any communities you're part of. Engage in the LaunchDay Slack. The influencer promotion helps, but your own network amplifies it.
