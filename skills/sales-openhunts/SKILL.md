## Step 3 — OpenHunts platform reference

### How OpenHunts works

OpenHunts is a product launch and discovery platform positioned as a Product Hunt alternative for indie makers. Products compete in **weekly voting cycles that refresh every Monday**, giving launches a full 7-day window of visibility — longer than daily-cycle platforms like Product Hunt or Firsto.

**Key difference**: Weekly launch cadence means your product competes for a full week, not just 24 hours. This gives smaller products more time to accumulate votes and visibility.

#### Launch flow
1. Create an account on OpenHunts
2. Submit your product: name, description, URL, screenshots
3. Choose a tier: Free (queue), Premium ($9.90), or Highlight ($20)
4. All launches go live on **Mondays** — weekly cycle
5. Products compete in weekly rankings through community voting
6. Top products get featured in newsletter and Twitter shoutouts

#### Pricing tiers

| Tier | Cost | Queue | Backlink | What you get |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | ~89 weeks, 20 slots/week | Dofollow for Top 3 only | Full week exposure, homepage featured |
| Premium | $9.90 | Next Monday, 25 slots/week | Guaranteed dofollow | 5x more exposure, full week visibility |
| Highlight | $20/week | Instant, pinned at top | Guaranteed dofollow | 10x exposure, guaranteed Twitter + newsletter shoutout |

#### Free backlink conditions (important!)

The free tier backlink is **not automatic**. You must finish in the **Top 3 weekly ranking** to earn a dofollow backlink. Unlike some other platforms, OpenHunts does not appear to require a badge on your website — just a Top 3 finish.

If you don't finish Top 3, you get the listing but NOT the dofollow backlink.

## Step 4 — Actionable guidance

### Submission checklist

| Step | What to do | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Prepare your listing | Clear name, problem-focused description, screenshots | First impression + weekly ranking position |
| 2. Choose your tier | Free (patient), Premium ($9.90), or Highlight ($20) | Determines backlink guarantee and visibility |
| 3. Time your submission | Launches go live on Mondays | Coordinate your community push for Monday |
| 4. Rally your network | Share your OpenHunts listing on launch day | Early votes drive weekly ranking position |
| 5. Engage the community | Comment on other products, respond to feedback | Community engagement improves visibility |
| 6. Share externally | Post your listing link on Twitter, communities | External traffic drives ranking |

### Which tier to choose

**Free ($0) when:**
- Budget is zero AND you can wait ~89 weeks (1.7 years)
- You're confident you can reach Top 3 weekly
- You want to submit and forget — it'll go live eventually

**Premium ($9.90) when:**
- You want a guaranteed dofollow backlink — no conditions
- You need to launch next Monday
- Best value for a guaranteed backlink among launch directories

**Highlight ($20/week) when:**
- You want maximum visibility — pinned at top of the page
- Guaranteed Twitter and newsletter shoutout matters for your launch
- You're coordinating a broader launch and want OpenHunts to be a high-impact moment

### Multi-platform launch strategy

OpenHunts works best as one platform in a broader launch:

1. **SoloPush + ProductBurst** — instant listings (Day 1)
2. **Huzzler Launch Arena** — submit Monday for weekly competition
3. **Open Launch Premium ($12)** — next-day launch with DR65 backlink (highest DR for the price)
4. **OpenHunts Premium ($9.90)** — next-Monday launch with DR50 backlink + full week voting
5. **Firsto Premium ($19.90)** — 24-hour launch with DR57+ backlink + permanent SEO page
6. **MicroLaunch** — 30-day leaderboard cycle
7. **CtrlAlt.cc + LaunchingNext** — curated directories (queue or pay to skip)
8. **Product Hunt** — save for main launch event

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and integration gotchas that may be outdated.*

- **~89 week free queue is extremely long.** This is one of the longest free queues of any launch directory — nearly 2 years. If timing matters at all, the $9.90 Premium is effectively mandatory.
- **Free backlink requires Top 3.** Regular free listings do not get a dofollow backlink. You must finish in the Top 3 weekly ranking to earn one.
- **Weekly cycle means slower results.** Your launch competes for a full week, which is good for accumulating votes but means you wait longer for your ranking result compared to daily-cycle platforms.
- **DR 50 is mid-range.** Higher than CtrlAlt.cc (DA30), ProductBurst (DR46), and SoloPush (DR46-47), but lower than Firsto (DR57), Open Launch (DR65), or Product Hunt (DR91).
- **No public API.** Everything is manual through the web interface.
- **No SEO Growth/article option.** Unlike Firsto ($149) and Open Launch ($59), OpenHunts doesn't offer editorial review articles for additional SEO value.
- **Small platform.** ~11K monthly visitors is modest. Don't expect significant direct traffic — the value is the backlink and category discovery.

## Troubleshooting

### Stuck in the ~89 week free queue
**Symptom**: Submitted for free weeks ago, product hasn't launched
**Cause**: OpenHunts limits free launches to 20 slots/week. With ~89 weeks of backlog, free submissions wait nearly 2 years.
**Solution**: Upgrade to Premium ($9.90) for next-Monday launch. This is one of the cheapest paid options across all directories. If budget is truly zero, use the wait time to submit to instant-listing platforms like SoloPush and ProductBurst where you'll go live immediately.

### Free listing but no dofollow backlink
**Symptom**: Product is listed on OpenHunts but backlink check shows nofollow
**Cause**: Free-tier dofollow backlinks require finishing in the Top 3 weekly ranking. Regular free listings get nofollow links.
**Solution**: If the backlink matters, upgrade to Premium ($9.90) for guaranteed dofollow. Or submit to directories with automatic dofollow backlinks: ProductBurst (DR46), SoloPush (DR46-47), LaunchingNext (DR51).

### Not getting votes or engagement
**Symptom**: Product launched but has few votes after several days
**Cause**: OpenHunts is a smaller platform (~11K monthly visitors). Organic discovery alone may not generate significant engagement.
**Solution**: Actively share your OpenHunts listing URL on Twitter, relevant communities, and with your network. Engage with other products on OpenHunts (comment, vote) to build reciprocal visibility. Consider the Highlight tier ($20) for guaranteed social/newsletter promotion. Set realistic expectations — most value comes from the backlink, not direct traffic.
