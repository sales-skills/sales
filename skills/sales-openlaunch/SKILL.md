## Step 3 — Open Launch platform reference

### How Open Launch works

Open Launch is the first complete open-source alternative to Product Hunt. Products launch daily, get upvoted by the community, and compete for daily/monthly rankings. The platform lists 1,000+ tools across AI, SaaS, Productivity, Marketing, and Design categories.

**Key difference**: Open source (TypeScript, GitHub repo with 298+ stars) and a tiered backlink system where free backlinks are conditional while paid backlinks are guaranteed.

#### Submission flow
1. Create an account on Open Launch
2. Submit your product: name, description, URL, screenshots
3. Choose a tier: Free (queue), Premium ($12, launch tomorrow), or SEO Growth ($59)
4. All launches happen at **8:00 AM UTC**
5. Community upvotes determine daily rankings
6. Daily and monthly winners get recognition

#### Pricing tiers

| Tier | Cost | Backlink | Queue | What you get |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | Conditional dofollow (Top 3 daily + display badge) | Queue wait | Homepage feature, standard launch |
| Premium | $12/launch | Guaranteed DR65 dofollow | Launch tomorrow | Next-day launch, 10 slots/day, homepage feature |
| SEO Growth | $59 (was $199) | Guaranteed DR65 dofollow + SEO article | 5-7 day article delivery | Dedicated "[Product] review" article, Google ranking strategy, premium launch |

#### Free backlink conditions (important!)

The free tier backlink is **not automatic**. You must meet BOTH conditions:
1. **Finish in Top 3 daily ranking** — your product must be one of the 3 most-upvoted products that day
2. **Display the Open Launch badge** on your website — they verify this

If you don't meet both conditions, you get the listing but NOT the dofollow backlink. This makes the free backlink significantly harder to earn than on other platforms.

#### Visibility system

| Monthly winners | Aggregated monthly performance | Extended recognition |

### Which tier to choose

**Free ($0) when:**
- Budget is zero AND you're confident you can reach Top 3 daily
- You're willing to display the Open Launch badge on your site
- You don't mind waiting in the queue
- The backlink isn't critical (you're submitting for visibility only)

**Premium ($12) when:**
- You want the DR65 backlink guaranteed — no conditions
- You need to control your launch timing (next day)
- You don't want to gamble on reaching Top 3
- Best value per dollar of any paid launch directory option

**SEO Growth ($59) when:**
- You want both the backlink AND long-term Google search traffic
- A dedicated "[Product] review" article adds credibility
- You're investing in SEO and want content that ranks
- You value the article as marketing collateral beyond just the backlink

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and integration gotchas that may be outdated.*

- **Free backlink is NOT automatic.** This is the most important gotcha. Unlike every other directory in this category, Open Launch's free backlink requires BOTH Top 3 daily ranking AND displaying their badge on your site. If you just want a guaranteed backlink, pay the $12.
- **$12 Premium is unusually cheap.** At $12 for a guaranteed DR65 dofollow backlink, this is the best backlink-per-dollar value in the launch directory ecosystem. A comparable DR65 backlink through outreach could cost $200+.
- **SEO Growth article uses AI.** The $59 article is created with "product testing and AI assistance" then reviewed by humans. Quality may vary. It's non-refundable once purchased.
- **10 Premium slots per day.** Premium launches are capped at 10/day. On busy days these could sell out — plan accordingly.
- **DR65 but DA21.** The DR (Ahrefs) and DA (Moz) metrics diverge significantly. DR65 is strong; DA21 is modest. Depending on which metric you track, the perceived value differs.
- **Traffic is modest.** Like most indie launch directories, don't expect Product Hunt-level visitor numbers. The value is primarily the backlink and listing presence.
- **Open source means community-run.** The platform is maintained by a small team of contributors. Feature development and support depend on community participation.
- **8:00 AM UTC launch time is fixed.** No flexibility on when your product appears. Coordinate your promotion schedule around this time.

## Troubleshooting

### Free listing but no dofollow backlink
**Symptom**: Product is listed on Open Launch but the backlink is nofollow or missing
**Cause**: Free-tier backlinks require BOTH conditions: (1) finishing Top 3 in daily ranking, and (2) displaying the Open Launch badge on your website. If either condition isn't met, you don't get the dofollow backlink.
**Solution**: If the backlink matters to you, the simplest fix is upgrading to Premium ($12) — it guarantees the DR65 dofollow backlink with no conditions. If you want to earn it free, boost your upvotes (share with your network at 8 AM UTC launch) and add the Open Launch badge to your site's footer.

### Product stuck in the free queue
**Symptom**: Submitted days/weeks ago but product hasn't launched yet
**Cause**: Free submissions enter a queue. Open Launch limits the number of daily launches to ensure each product gets visibility, so the queue backs up.
**Solution**: Upgrade to Premium ($12) for next-day launch. The 10 daily premium slots rarely sell out. If budget is tight, be patient — your product will eventually launch. Use the wait time to refine your listing and prepare your promotion strategy for launch day.

### Low upvotes despite being on the daily feed
**Symptom**: Product launched but barely got any upvotes
**Cause**: Open Launch has modest organic traffic. Without external promotion, your listing relies on the platform's existing user base, which is smaller than Product Hunt.
**Solution**: Don't rely on organic discovery. Share your Open Launch page at exactly 8:00 AM UTC when your listing goes live. Tweet it, post in relevant communities, tell your email list. Early upvote momentum determines your daily ranking position. Engage with every comment. Consider the streak system to build your profile presence over time.
