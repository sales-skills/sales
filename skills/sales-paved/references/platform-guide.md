# Paved Platform Reference

## Platform overview

Paved is a newsletter sponsorship marketplace connecting advertisers with 3,000+ newsletter publishers reaching 253 million+ subscribers. Founded by John McLaughlin, headquartered in New York (300 Park Avenue), and acquired by Redbrick in March 2025. Major publisher partners include NYT, NBC, Bloomberg, TechCrunch, Fortune, Flipboard, and Dribbble. Paved serves 8,000+ newsletter creators and brands like Uber, DoorDash, Salesforce, Gong, and Dropbox.

## Key modules

### Marketplace
The core discovery engine. Advertisers browse hundreds of newsletters by category, niche, and audience size. Each listing shows verified subscriber count, open rates, estimated clicks, CPC range, audience demographics, and reviews from previous sponsors.

**For publishers**: Create a listing with your newsletter details, audience demographics, and rate card. Paved charges 30% commission on Marketplace sponsorships.

**For advertisers**: Free to browse and book. No subscription or platform fees. Search by topic vertical, audience size, engagement metrics, and budget range.

### Ad Network
Programmatic, CPC-based newsletter ad placement. Automated — publishers insert a code snippet in their newsletter template, and Paved serves targeted native ads.

**Key details**:
- Cost-per-click (CPC) pricing model
- Average payout: ~$1.45 per click (as of research date)
- No minimum budget for advertisers
- Curated network focused on technical and high-net-worth audiences
- Publishers need to add an HTML placement to their ESP template

**Best for**: Publishers who want passive income without managing sponsor relationships. Advertisers who want scale without manual outreach.

### Booker
Direct sponsorship workflow management. Streamlines the process of managing direct sponsor relationships — handling invoices, insertion orders, contracts, and payouts.

**Use case**: Publishers who have their own sponsors but want Paved to handle the operational overhead (billing, contracts, scheduling).

### Radar
Lead enrichment tool for sponsorship pipelines. Helps publishers identify potential sponsors and understand advertiser activity in the newsletter space.

**Use case**: Publishers who want to do outbound sponsor outreach with data-backed targeting.

## Pricing and limits

### For advertisers
- **Platform fee**: None — completely free
- **Marketplace**: Pay per placement (negotiate with publisher, or accept listed rates)
- **Ad Network**: CPC bidding model, no minimum budget
- **Typical CPM range**: $15–$30 per 1,000 opens (varies by niche)

### For publishers
- **Marketplace commission**: 30% of sponsorship revenue
- **Ad Network**: Revenue share (CPC-based, ~$1.45 avg per click)
- **Minimum payout**: $25
- **Payment methods**: PayPal or direct bank transfer
- **Payment schedule**: Monthly
- **Minimum subscriber requirement**: 5,000 subscribers to list on Marketplace

### Ad formats supported
- Sponsored email ads (native placement within newsletter)
- Dedicated email sponsorships (entire email from sponsor)
- Sponsored blog content
- Programmatic ads (via Ad Network)

## Workflow setup

### Publisher onboarding
1. **Sign up** at paved.com
2. **Connect your ESP** (Profile → Newsletter Settings) for verified metrics
3. **Complete your media kit** — subscriber count, demographics, niche, engagement rates, past sponsors
4. **Set your rates** — CPM-based or flat rate per placement
5. **Choose channels**: Marketplace (curated bookings), Ad Network (programmatic), or both
6. **For Ad Network**: Add the HTML code snippet to your newsletter template in your ESP
7. **Wait for bookings** or use Radar to do outbound sponsor outreach

### Advertiser workflow
1. **Sign up** at paved.com (free)
2. **Browse Marketplace** — filter by category, audience size, engagement, budget
3. **Review newsletter listings** — check verified stats, past sponsor reviews, audience demographics
4. **Book placements** — request dates, submit ad creative, confirm pricing
5. **Track performance** — real-time analytics for opens, clicks, conversions
6. **Optimize** — double down on high-performing newsletters, test different ad creative

### Media kit optimization (for publishers)
A strong media kit is the difference between getting booked and being ignored:

- **Subscriber count**: Verified via ESP integration (more credible than self-reported)
- **Open rate**: Show recent 30-day average (aim for 35%+ for premium positioning)
- **Audience demographics**: Industry, job titles, company size, geography — the more specific, the better for niche advertisers
- **Past sponsor logos**: Social proof from recognizable brands
- **Case study data**: Click-through rates and conversions from past campaigns
- **Rate card**: Clear pricing for different placement types (primary, secondary, dedicated)

## Deep dives

### Marketplace vs Ad Network decision

| Factor | Marketplace | Ad Network |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue model | Flat rate / CPM | CPC |
| Revenue per placement | Higher (premium) | Lower (volume) |
| Effort | Manual (review/approve each sponsor) | Automated (code snippet) |
| Commission | 30% | Revenue share |
| Control | Full (choose sponsors) | Limited (Paved serves ads) |
| Best for | Established newsletters with engaged audience | Growing newsletters wanting passive income |

**Recommendation**: Use both. Marketplace for premium placements (1-2 per issue), Ad Network to fill remaining inventory.

### Pricing your newsletter sponsorships on Paved

**CPM benchmarks by niche** (cost per 1,000 opens):
- B2B / SaaS / Tech: $50–$100+
- Finance / Investing: $50–$100+
- Marketing / Growth: $30–$60
- General business: $25–$50
- Lifestyle / General interest: $15–$30

**Formula**: Subscribers × open rate × CPM / 1,000 = rate per placement
**Example**: 10,000 subscribers × 45% open rate × $50 CPM / 1,000 = $225/placement (before Paved's 30% cut, you'd net ~$157.50)
