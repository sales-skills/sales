## Step 4 — Actionable guidance

### When to submit to PitchWall

**Submit if:**
- Your product is AI-powered or an AI tool (PitchWall's primary focus)
- You want newsletter exposure to 45K+ tech-savvy subscribers
- You're doing a comprehensive directory submission campaign (free tier, low effort)
- You're willing to wait 30+ days for the free tier

**Deprioritize if:**
- Your product is not AI-related (PitchWall curates AI products specifically)
- Your primary goal is SEO backlinks (backlink type is disputed, traffic is low)
- You need a listing live quickly and don't want to pay $99

### Optimizing your listing

1. **Product name and tagline** — lead with the problem you solve, not the tech. PitchWall's audience scans for tools that solve specific problems.
2. **Category tags** — use relevant tags (AI, Productivity, Marketing, Design, Development, SaaS) to improve discovery and interlinking.
3. **Screenshots and video** — PitchWall supports YouTube video embedding. A demo video significantly increases engagement.
4. **Social links** — add all social profiles. PitchWall displays these on your product page.
5. **Guest blog post** — consider submitting a guest post for an additional dofollow backlink (50 DA, 60 DR). This is separate from the product listing.

### Submission sequencing

In a multi-directory launch, PitchWall fits in **Phase 3 (free editorial queues)** — submit on Day 1 alongside other free editorial submissions (Startup Stash, CtrlAlt.cc, MicroLaunch, etc.). It will go live after manual review, typically 30+ days for free tier.

If timing matters and your product is AI-focused, the Premium tier ($99) gets 1-day review and lets you choose your launch date — useful for coordinating with a Product Hunt or Hacker News launch.

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and integration gotchas that may be outdated.*

- **Backlink type is disputed.** PitchWall's own submission page claims "do-follow backlink" with 50 DA and 60 DR. However, third-party directory databases (launchdirectories.com) report the backlinks as nofollow. Always inspect the HTML of your listing page after publication to verify. The guest blog post backlink is separately confirmed as dofollow.
- **30+ day free queue.** The free tier has a 30+ day waiting period before publication. If you need a specific launch date, you must pay $99 for Premium.
- **AI focus.** PitchWall curates "new AI products & tools every day." Non-AI products may be deprioritized or rejected during manual review.
- **Traffic is modest.** Third-party estimates suggest ~1K monthly visits (Ahrefs). PitchWall's main distribution channel is the newsletter (45K+ subscribers), not organic website traffic.
- **Premium pricing is high for the DR.** At $99, PitchWall's Premium tier is more expensive than most directory paid options (Open Launch $12, Fazier $39, TinyLaunch $39) and the DR is comparable or lower. The value proposition is newsletter exposure, not backlink strength.
- **Manual review for all tiers.** Even Premium submissions go through manual review. There's no guaranteed acceptance — the team checks for legitimacy and relevance.
- **Advertising metrics are self-reported.** Performance estimates (120-200 clicks for newsletter sponsor, 200-600 for dedicated newsletter) come from PitchWall's own advertising page. Independent verification of these numbers is not available.

## Troubleshooting

### Submitted free listing but no response after 30 days
**Symptom**: Filled out the free submission form and haven't heard back after the stated waiting period
**Cause**: PitchWall manually reviews all submissions. The 30+ day estimate is a minimum — actual turnaround may be longer. AI-relevant products may be prioritized.
**Solution**: Email support@pitchwall.co to check on your submission status. If no response after 2 weeks of follow-up, deprioritize and focus on directories with faster turnaround (ProductBurst, SoloPush, JustGotFound — all instant). Consider upgrading to Premium ($99) if timing matters.

### Listing shows nofollow backlink despite dofollow claims
**Symptom**: Inspected the HTML and your listing page has `rel="nofollow"` on the backlink
**Cause**: PitchWall claims dofollow backlinks, but third-party reports conflict. The actual implementation may differ from marketing claims, or may vary by tier.
**Solution**: If dofollow backlinks are critical for your SEO strategy, contact support@pitchwall.co to ask about their backlink policy. Consider submitting a guest blog post — the blog backlinks are more reliably dofollow. For guaranteed dofollow directories, prioritize Fazier (DR81), Uneed (DR74), or Open Launch (DR65).

### Product rejected during manual review
**Symptom**: Submission was rejected or no listing appeared after Premium payment
**Cause**: PitchWall curates for AI products specifically. Non-AI products, incomplete landing pages, or products without clear value propositions may be rejected.
**Solution**: Ensure your product has a clear AI angle in the description and landing page. If your product isn't AI-focused, submit to general directories instead (Uneed, SideProjectors, Open Launch). If Premium was paid and listing rejected, contact support@pitchwall.co for a refund or guidance on resubmission.
