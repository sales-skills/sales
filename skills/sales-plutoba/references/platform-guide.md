# PlutoBa Platform Reference

## Platform overview

PlutoBa is an AI-powered creator intelligence platform positioned as "The Trust Layer for Influencer Partnerships." Launched March 2026, it serves DTC brands, marketing agencies, and content creators. It is explicitly **not a discovery tool** — it's a due diligence engine for vetting creators you've already identified through other channels.

PlutoBa analyzes creators across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube, generating a comprehensive PlutoBa Score (0-100) from 100+ posts and 300+ comments in under two minutes.

## PlutoBa Score — 7 dimensions

Every assessment produces scores across these dimensions:

1. **Audience Authenticity** — detects fake/purchased followers, bot accounts, inorganic growth patterns
2. **Comment Authenticity** — identifies bot-generated comments, engagement pods, purchased engagement
3. **Engagement Quality** — measures genuine interaction quality beyond simple like/comment counts
4. **Brand Safety** — scans historical content for profanity, controversial topics, competitor mentions, red-flag content
5. **Content Consistency** — evaluates posting frequency, content quality over time, niche adherence
6. **Posting Patterns** — analyzes posting cadence, peak times, regularity, growth trajectory
7. **Rate Benchmarking** — compares the creator's pricing to market rates for their tier, niche, and platform

## Assessment types

### Standard Assessment
- Faster, lighter analysis
- Good for initial screening and filtering
- Included in higher quantities in all plans

### Deep Assessment
- Analyzes 100+ posts and 300+ comments
- Full 7-dimension scoring
- AI-generated campaign brief with rate benchmarks, content guidelines, and deliverable recommendations
- Limited quantities per plan — reserve for high-value partnerships

## Key modules

### Creator CRM
- Manage influencer relationships within PlutoBa
- Capacity varies by plan (25 to 500 creators for brands, more for agencies)
- Campaign tracking with active campaign limits per plan
- Profile fetches for quick lookups (100 to 1,000 per plan)

### AI Outreach
- AI-powered personalized creator outreach messages
- References specific content the creator has posted
- Generates campaign briefs with rate benchmarks and deliverable recommendations
- Available on all brand/agency plans

### Campaign Briefs (auto-generated)
- Rate benchmarks by tier and niche
- Content guidelines tailored to the creator's style
- Deliverable recommendations based on the campaign's goals
- Generated automatically from Deep Assessments

## Creator-facing features

PlutoBa has a two-sided model:

**For creators:**
- Free verified contact page at plutoba.com/@handle — acts as a professional landing page for brand inquiries
- AI brand classification on every incoming inquiry — helps creators evaluate if a brand is legitimate
- Auto-acknowledgement for incoming inquiries
- Scam and low-quality brand detection (Pro only)
- Rate benchmarking to know their market value (Pro only)
- PlutoBa Verified badge (Pro only) — signals credibility to brands

This two-sided model means brands using PlutoBa may find that some creators are already on the platform with verified profiles, which adds a trust signal to the vetting process.

## Competitive positioning

PlutoBa differentiates from established platforms:

- **vs HypeAuditor**: HypeAuditor is discovery + analytics (223M+ profiles, 53 fraud patterns). PlutoBa is vetting-only with a two-sided creator model and lower price entry ($69 vs $299).
- **vs Viral Nation Secure**: Secure is enterprise brand safety monitoring at scale. PlutoBa is point-in-time vetting for individual creator assessments.
- **vs trendHERO**: trendHERO is Instagram-only with AQS scoring. PlutoBa covers TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube with 7-dimension scoring.
- **vs Modash/influData**: These are primarily discovery platforms. PlutoBa deliberately skips discovery to focus on due diligence.
