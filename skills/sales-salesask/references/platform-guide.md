# Sales Ask Platform Reference

## Platform overview

Sales Ask is an AI-powered sales coaching platform purpose-built for home services and trades businesses. It uses "Coach Dean," an AI agent that records, transcribes, and analyzes in-person and phone sales conversations, then texts reps immediate, specific feedback. Unlike Rilla (post-appointment dashboards) or Siro (multi-industry horizontal coaching), Sales Ask is exclusively focused on the home services vertical with native integrations into the trade-specific CRM stack (ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro).

**Target audience**: HVAC, roofing, plumbing, home building, remodeling, windows/doors, dental/DSO, chimney services. Teams of 5-50+ reps.

**Key differentiators**:
- **Active coaching** — Coach Dean texts reps actionable feedback immediately after each interaction, not a passive dashboard requiring manager review
- **Full-funnel coverage** — records both call center (CSR/phone) and field (in-home) conversations, unlike Rilla/Siro which are field-only
- **Native trade CRM integrations** — ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro link recordings directly to revenue tickets for ROI proof
- **Process Adherence Scoring** — contextual analysis of whether reps followed the sales process, not just keyword detection or talk metrics

## Key modules

### Coach Dean AI Agent
- Reviews 100% of recorded interactions (in-person and phone)
- Scores key sales steps against your custom criteria
- Texts reps immediate, specific feedback after each appointment
- Identifies "hinge moments" — specific points where deals were won/lost, upsells missed, or financing skipped
- Example feedback: "You skipped financing options on this appointment — 37% of contractors who present financing see higher ticket values"

### Recording & Transcription
- Mobile app records in-person conversations
- Supports both in-person field sales and virtual/phone sales
- Automatic transcription with AI analysis
- AI-generated meeting summaries including scope, timeline, budget, competitors mentioned

### Process Adherence Scoring
- Goes beyond keywords and talk metrics — analyzes whether reps followed the actual sales process
- Customizable scoring criteria aligned to your company methodology
- Tracks: did the rep present multiple options? Mention financing? Walk through warranty? Address common objections?
- Scores flagged with specific improvement suggestions

### Virtual Ridealongs
- Managers coach remotely through recorded sales interactions
- No physical travel required — review any appointment from anywhere
- Equivalent of riding along on every appointment without the time/cost
- Enables consistent coaching across geographically dispersed teams

### AI Roleplay ("The Lab")
- Reps practice sales conversations in a simulated environment
- Real-time skill improvement tracking
- Practice specific scenarios: presenting options, handling objections, introducing financing
- Safe environment to practice before live appointments

### Pipeline Analytics
- Identifies winning patterns across the team
- Shows which sales steps correlate with higher close rates and ticket values
- Compares individual rep performance to team benchmarks
- Tracks improvement over time after coaching interventions

### Automated Follow-up
- AI composes follow-up emails and texts based on appointment content
- Captures next steps, commitments, and action items from conversations
- Reduces admin time — Sales Ask claims 40% less manual work

## Workflow setup

### Initial setup (under 2 hours)
1. Install mobile app on each rep's phone
2. Configure recording consent disclosure (check your state requirements)
3. Connect CRM (ServiceTitan/Jobber/Housecall Pro/Salesforce/Lasso)
4. Customize Coach Dean's scoring criteria for your sales process
5. Upload your sales script or let Coach Dean learn from top performers
6. Set up the first virtual ridealong for the sales manager

### Ongoing coaching workflow
1. Rep starts appointment → app records automatically
2. Coach Dean transcribes, analyzes, and scores the conversation
3. Rep receives text message with specific coaching feedback
4. Manager reviews dashboard for team-wide patterns
5. Manager runs virtual ridealongs on flagged conversations
6. Reps practice weak areas in The Lab (AI roleplay)
7. Pipeline analytics track improvement over time
