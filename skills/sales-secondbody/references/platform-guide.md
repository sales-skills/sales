# SecondBody Platform Reference

## Platform overview

SecondBody is a voice-first AI sales training and coaching platform that helps reps practice real conversations daily, receive personalized AI coaching, and build confidence before customer calls. Positioned as "Duolingo for B2B sales conversations" — daily habit-building over quarterly certification. Target audience: SDR/BDR teams, AE teams, distributed sales forces, and organizations wanting to scale coaching beyond what managers can deliver 1:1. Founded 2023.

## Key modules

### AI Roleplay Practice
- Voice-first adaptive conversations (not chatbot-with-voice-bolted-on)
- Adjusts difficulty based on rep performance in real time
- Tests objection handling, discovery frameworks, and closing techniques
- Custom AI personas that match your buyer profiles (Pro)
- Upload your own objection library from real calls (Pro)
- Methodology-native scoring: SPIN, MEDDIC, Sandler mapped to rubrics
- Confidence scoring before customer calls

### Rory AI Coach
- Daily async coaching integrated into workflow
- Pulls context from CRM data, call recordings, pricing docs, company knowledge bases
- Answers using organization-specific playbooks (not generic training)
- Pre-1:1 briefing for managers — targeted coaching summaries per rep
- 60 minutes/month on Pro tier

### Manager Analytics
- Team performance heatmaps showing skill gaps across the team
- Behavioral pattern tracking: objection handling, discovery, closing
- Coaching assignment recommendations based on heatmap data
- Call conversion rate lift monitoring
- Identifies who needs coaching and on what

### Gamification & Engagement
- Daily streaks — teams using streaks practice 3-4x more
- Team challenges and weekly quests
- Leaderboards
- VS Mode — 1:1 competitions between reps
- Call Blitz — 15-minute focused practice sessions

### Gap Analysis & QA
- Audits real calls to identify where conversations break down
- Identifies the top 8 objections killing deals
- Conversation QA and competency tracking
- Connects training gaps to actual performance data

## Use cases

## Deep dives

### Setting up Fathom integration
The Fathom integration crowdsources objections directly from real calls. Every objection Fathom captures becomes a ready-to-use practice scenario distributed to reps for roleplay. This creates a closed loop between what prospects actually say and what reps practice.

### Building effective AI personas
1. Start with your top 3 buyer personas (not all of them)
2. Upload real objections from call recordings — don't write generic ones
3. Set difficulty to match the actual conversation dynamics your reps face
4. Update personas monthly as objection patterns shift

### Manager workflow with heatmaps
1. Review team heatmap weekly — look for consistent red zones across reps (systemic) vs individual red zones (coaching targets)
2. Use pre-1:1 briefings from Rory to focus coaching conversations
3. Assign specific roleplay scenarios based on skill gaps
4. Track streak data to identify engagement drops before they become performance drops
