### Free vs paid backlink behavior

This is the key distinction on Super Launch:

| Tier | Cost | Front page | Backlink | Dofollow? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free submission | $0 | 7 days | Yes | **No** — nofollow |
| Paid/featured | Varies | 7 days | Yes | **Yes** — dofollow (DR59) |
| Advertising | $49/month | Carousel on all pages | Yes | Unknown — likely dofollow |

**Important**: LaunchDirectories reports Super Launch as "dofollow, free" generically, but the actual page data shows free listings get `dofollow: false` and paid listings get `dofollow: true`. The free tier gives you a nofollow backlink. If you need a dofollow backlink, you'll need a paid listing.

### Submission process

1. **Create an account** at superlaun.ch
2. **Prepare materials**: product description, screenshots, founder story
3. **Submit your product** following the platform's guidelines
4. **Your product appears on the front page for 7 days** — guaranteed, no editorial gatekeeping
5. **Engage with the community** — respond to votes and comments
6. **Aim for the Top 50 Leaderboard** — high vote counts get you on the permanent leaderboard

### When Super Launch makes sense

**Good fit:**
- You want guaranteed 7-day visibility with no editorial queue
- You're doing a broad directory sweep and want another listing
- You can afford $49/month for a DR59 dofollow backlink via advertising

**Not ideal:**
- Your primary goal is a free dofollow backlink — free tier is nofollow. Use SoloPush, ProductBurst, or Open Launch instead
- You need a permanent always-visible listing — 7-day front page then you rotate off (though you remain in the directory)

## Step 4 — Actionable guidance

### For users submitting (free tier)

1. Create account at superlaun.ch
2. Prepare: clear product name, problem-focused tagline, screenshots of product in action
3. Submit your product
4. Add UTM tracking: `?utm_source=superlaunch&utm_medium=directory&utm_campaign=launch`
5. Engage during your 7-day window — ask your network to vote
6. After 7 days, your product moves off the front page but stays in the directory and can appear on the leaderboard

**Note**: The free backlink is nofollow. If dofollow backlinks are your goal, prioritize other directories first (SoloPush, ProductBurst, Open Launch).

### For users considering the $49/month ad

**Compare with alternatives:**
- **$49/month on Super Launch** = carousel ad across all pages + likely dofollow backlink (DR59)
- **$12 on Open Launch** = guaranteed DR65 dofollow backlink + next-day launch (one-time, not monthly)
- **$39 on CtrlAlt.cc** = 1-week homepage feature + dofollow backlink (one-time)
- **$39/month on MicroLaunch Pro** = skip queue + 2x visibility + DR59 backlink

**Verdict**: The $49/month ad is expensive compared to one-time alternatives. Unless you want ongoing ad visibility on Super Launch specifically, one-time paid options at other directories deliver better backlink value per dollar.

### For multi-directory launchers

Super Launch fits into **Phase 1 — Instant listings** as a free, no-queue submission. The nofollow backlink is a downside compared to SoloPush and ProductBurst (both free dofollow), but the 7-day guaranteed front page is a nice perk. Submit it alongside other instant platforms on Day 1.

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about backlink type and pricing that may change.*

- **Free tier backlinks are nofollow.** Despite some sources reporting "dofollow, free," the actual page data shows free listings get nofollow links. Only paid/featured listings get dofollow. Always verify after your listing goes live.
- **DR59 is strong but only for paid.** DR59 would rank Super Launch among the top directories — but only if you get the dofollow variant, which requires paying.
- **$49/month is recurring.** Unlike one-time payments at Open Launch ($12) or CtrlAlt.cc ($39), the Super Launch ad is a monthly subscription. Cancel if you only need the backlink.
- **7-day front page is nice but limited.** After 7 days, your product rotates off the homepage. You remain in the directory but visibility drops significantly.
- **10 ad slots per month.** If all 10 slots are taken, you can't purchase advertising until the next month.
- **No editorial queue — low barrier.** This means less curation, so the quality of co-listed products varies more than on editorial platforms.

- **Self-improving**: If you discover something not covered here, append it to `references/learnings.md` with today's date.

## Troubleshooting

### Backlink shows as nofollow after submitting for free
**Symptom**: Listed on Super Launch but the backlink is nofollow
**Cause**: Free submissions get nofollow backlinks. Only paid/featured listings get dofollow.
**Solution**: This is expected behavior. If you need a dofollow backlink from Super Launch, you'll need to purchase advertising ($49/month). For free dofollow backlinks, use SoloPush, ProductBurst, or other platforms listed in `/sales-launch-directory`.

### Product not visible after 7 days
**Symptom**: Product was on the front page but now can't be found
**Cause**: The 7-day front page placement expired. Your product moves to the general directory but is no longer prominently featured.
**Solution**: Your product is still listed — it just isn't on the homepage anymore. If you accumulated enough votes, you may appear on the Top 50 Leaderboard. To regain visibility, consider the $49/month advertising option or focus on other directories where your listing is permanent.

### Low vote count during 7-day window
**Symptom**: Product on the front page but getting very few votes
**Cause**: Super Launch has unknown traffic levels, and your product may not be in a popular category
**Solution**: Promote your Super Launch listing on your own channels (Twitter, email list, communities). Ask early users to vote. The 7-day window is guaranteed regardless of vote count — use it for exposure even if votes are low. Focus your backlink efforts on higher-traffic directories.
