## Step 3 — SWAI platform reference

### Core concept: Goal-driven campaigns

SWAI's differentiator is autonomous campaign execution. You set a goal in plain English — "I need more qualified leads", "I want to launch a webinar", "I want to sell my new service" — and SWAI builds, runs, and optimizes the entire funnel.

**How it works:**
1. **Set a goal** — describe what you want in natural language
2. **SWAI plans** — AI creates a campaign strategy, selects channels, builds assets
3. **SWAI executes** — landing pages, emails, ads, chat — deployed automatically
4. **SWAI optimizes** — continuous A/B testing and performance tuning

### Landing pages

SWAI recently rebuilt its landing page experience (no longer uses a third-party builder):
- **Fully customizable** — real control over design, content, and layout
- **Custom forms powered by AI agents** — forms that qualify and route leads intelligently
- **Script injection** — add tracking pixels, custom JavaScript, advanced features
- **Brand-tailored** — AI generates pages matched to each brand's identity
- **No coding required** — visual builder for non-technical users

### AI chat widget

- **Website embed** — add AI-powered chat to any website for lead capture
- **AI-driven conversations** — handles initial qualification, answers FAQs, captures contact info
- **Appointment scheduling** — integrates with Cal.com for booking meetings directly from chat
- **24/7 availability** — AI handles conversations when team is offline

### CRM

- **Contact management** — track contacts through the customer journey
- **AI tagging** — automatic contact categorization based on behavior and engagement (full AI tagging on Elite plan)
- **Segmentation** — filter and target contacts by attributes and behavior
- **Contact limits** — vary by plan (unlimited on Elite)

### Email marketing

- **AI-generated campaigns** — SWAI creates email content, subject lines, and send schedules
- **Automation** — trigger-based sequences (welcome, nurture, re-engagement)
- **Personalization** — dynamic content based on contact data and behavior
- **Part of the autonomous system** — email campaigns are coordinated with landing pages, chat, and ads as part of the overall goal

### AI assistants

SWAI provides AI assistants for multiple business functions:

- **Customer service** — answers support questions, routes complex issues
- **Lead generation** — identifies and captures new prospects

### Content creation

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and integration gotchas that may be outdated.*

- **Don't expect full control on lower plans.** AI tagging, unlimited contacts, and dedicated support are gated to higher tiers. Ask about the user's plan before recommending features.
- **SWAI is not a traditional marketing tool.** Users coming from Mailchimp or ActiveCampaign expect manual campaign builders. SWAI's value is autonomous execution — setting goals and letting AI handle the rest. Fighting the autonomous approach reduces value.
- **Landing page builder was recently rebuilt.** If the user references third-party landing page issues or old workflows, note that SWAI now generates pages natively with full customization.
- **No public API documented.** As of research, SWAI does not have public API documentation. Users needing programmatic access should contact SWAI support.
- **Integrations are limited compared to mature platforms.** SWAI integrates with Google, Facebook, Cal.com, and Rewardful — but doesn't have native CRM connectors (Salesforce, HubSpot) or Zapier. Users needing complex integrations may need to combine SWAI with other tools.

## Troubleshooting

### Campaign not generating results
**Symptom**: AI campaign is running but not producing leads or conversions
**Cause**: Goal may be too vague, target audience not well-defined, or insufficient optimization time
**Solution**: Refine the goal with more specific language (include target audience, desired action, and timeline). Check that landing pages and chat widget are receiving traffic. Allow at least 1-2 weeks for AI optimization to take effect.

### Landing page forms not capturing leads
**Symptom**: Page gets traffic but form submissions are low
**Cause**: Form is too long, AI agent questions are unclear, or page design doesn't drive action
**Solution**: Reduce form fields to essentials (name, email, key qualifier). Review AI agent conversation flow for clarity. Ensure the page has a clear value proposition above the fold.

### Chat widget not appearing on website
**Symptom**: Widget installed but doesn't show on the site
**Cause**: Script not properly embedded, caching issue, or content security policy blocking
**Solution**: Verify the embed script is in the page's `<head>` or `<body>`. Clear site cache and CDN cache. Check browser console for CSP errors — add SWAI's domain to your CSP allow list. Test in incognito mode to rule out ad blockers.
