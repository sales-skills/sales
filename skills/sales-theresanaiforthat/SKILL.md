## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about plan-gated features and pricing that may be outdated.*

- **No free submission path (mostly).** The standard submission is $347. The only free option is a monthly X thread where Andrei picks one tool — low odds, unpredictable timing.
- **$300 PPC bonus requires TAAFT-first launch.** Your tool must not have appeared on any other platform before TAAFT. Plan your submission sequence accordingly — submit to TAAFT before other directories.
- **Manually reviewed.** Average 1-2 days, but no SLA. Budget extra time if coordinating a multi-platform launch.
- **Featured ad pricing is bid-based.** Higher bids = higher position. There's no fixed rate card — test with small bids first.
- **Highlighted listing pricing is not public.** Contact TAAFT directly for current rates.
- **Listings don't expire.** Once listed, your tool stays permanently. The $347 is a one-time fee.
- **AI tools only.** Non-AI products will be rejected with a refund. If your tool has AI features but isn't primarily AI, emphasize the AI angle.
- **Newsletter is massive (1M+ subscribers) but inclusion is editorial.** You can't buy newsletter placement separately from listing — it's based on editorial selection.

## Troubleshooting

### Submission rejected or no response after 3+ days
**Symptom**: Paid $347 but tool isn't listed and no update
**Cause**: Manual review queue. Could be delayed, or tool may not meet AI-tool criteria.
**Solution**: Wait up to 5 business days. If still no update, reach out via the contact email or Andrei's Twitter DMs (@imakecoolsites). TAAFT automatically refunds if the tool isn't a good fit.

### Listing gets few clicks despite high traffic
**Symptom**: Listed on TAAFT but referral traffic is low
**Cause**: TAAFT is task-based — users search for specific problems. If your tool's description doesn't match common search queries, it won't surface.
**Solution**: Rewrite your description to lead with the task/problem your tool solves, using the exact words users would type. Test by searching TAAFT for your tool's use case — if it doesn't appear in results, your description needs work. Consider running Featured ads for targeted visibility.

### Not sure if the $300 PPC bonus applied
**Symptom**: Submitted as TAAFT-first but can't see the bonus
**Cause**: The bonus is auto-applied to qualifying tools after submission. It may not be immediately visible.
**Solution**: Check your TAAFT account for PPC credit balance. The bonus requires that your tool hasn't appeared on any other platform before the TAAFT submission. If it was listed elsewhere first (even briefly), the bonus won't apply.
