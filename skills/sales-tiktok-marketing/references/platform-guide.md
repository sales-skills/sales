# TikTok Marketing Platform Guide

## TikTok algorithm fundamentals

TikTok's algorithm surfaces content based on **engagement signals**, not follower count. Key mechanics:

- **First 3 seconds are critical** — the algorithm tests new videos with a small audience (~200-500 views). If watch-through rate, likes, comments, shares, and saves are strong, it expands distribution
- **Watch-through rate is king** — a 60% completion rate on a 30-second video signals strong content. Longer videos need even higher engagement to compensate
- **Content > account size** — accounts with 0 followers can go viral. The algorithm tests content quality independently of creator reputation
- **Batch testing** — TikTok pushes content in waves. A video may get 500 views, stall, then suddenly spike 24-72 hours later if engagement metrics held
- **Hashtags signal topic, not reach** — use 3-5 relevant hashtags to help TikTok categorize content. Viral hashtags (#fyp, #foryou) have no proven benefit

## Organic growth strategy

**Content strategy:**
- **Ideal video length**: 21-34 seconds for optimal completion rate. Under 60 seconds for new accounts.
- **Posting cadence**: 4-6 posts per week minimum. Consistency matters more than volume. 8+ weeks before expecting traction.
- **Content pillars**: educational (how-to, tips), entertaining (trends, humor), behind-the-scenes, user testimonials
- **Hook formula**: Question → Value → CTA. Open with a question or bold statement in first 1-2 seconds.
- **Trending audio**: Use trending sounds within 48 hours of emergence. TikTok prioritizes content using trending audio.
- **Native formats**: Duets, Stitches, Green Screen, text overlays, voiceover. Algorithm favors native TikTok features over polished production.

**Growth timeline (realistic expectations):**
- **Weeks 1-4**: Establish content cadence, test content pillars, gather baseline data. Expect 200-2K views/video.
- **Weeks 5-8**: Double down on what works. Optimize hooks and formats based on data. Expect 1K-10K views on top performers.
- **Months 3-6**: Consistent growth if content-market fit is found. Expect 10K-50K followers if posting 5x/week with 2-3 viral-ish videos.
- **60-90 days** before strategy produces reliable, scalable results.

**Profile optimization:**
- Username: brand name, searchable, no underscores if possible
- Bio: what you do + who it's for + CTA (link in bio, shop, etc.)
- Profile picture: logo or recognizable face
- Pinned videos: best 3 videos that represent your content pillars
- Business vs Creator account: Creator gets more trending sounds; Business gets analytics and ads. Most brands start Business, switch to Creator for organic reach.

## TikTok paid ads

**Campaign objectives:**
| Objective | Best for | Key metric |
|---|---|---|
| **Reach** | Brand awareness, new product launches | CPM ($3-8 typical) |
| **Traffic** | Website visits, landing page | CPC ($0.50-2.00) |
| **Video views** | Content distribution, retargeting pool building | CPV ($0.01-0.05) |
| **App installs** | Mobile apps, games | CPI ($1-5, varies by category) |
| **Conversions** | Ecommerce sales, lead gen | ROAS (target 3-5x for ecommerce) |
| **Lead generation** | B2B, high-consideration products | CPL ($5-30) |

**Ad formats:**
- **In-Feed Ads** — standard video ads in the For You feed (5-60 seconds)
- **Spark Ads** — boost existing organic posts or creator content as ads. 2-3x higher engagement than standard in-feed. Best for authentic feel.
- **TopView** — full-screen video on app open (premium placement, $50K+ minimum)
- **Branded Hashtag Challenge** — sponsored challenge page ($150K+ package)
- **Branded Effects** — AR filters and stickers ($80K+)
- **TikTok Shop Ads** — product listing ads in the shop tab

**Creative best practices for ads:**
- First 3 seconds: hook with a problem, question, or bold claim
- Native feel: shoot on phone, use trending audio, avoid overproduction
- UGC-style performs 2-3x better than polished brand creative
- 9:16 vertical, text safe zone, captions (85% watch with sound off at least sometimes)
- Test 3-5 creative variants per ad group. Refresh creative every 7-14 days (creative fatigue hits fast on TikTok)

**Targeting:**
- **Interest targeting** — TikTok's interest categories (broader than Meta's)
- **Behavior targeting** — video interactions, creator interactions, hashtag interactions
- **Custom audiences** — website pixel, app events, customer lists, engagement audiences
- **Lookalike audiences** — based on custom audiences (1%, 2%, 5%, 10%)
- **Smart targeting** — TikTok's algorithm optimizes targeting automatically (often outperforms manual targeting for conversions)

**Budget and bidding:**
- Minimum $50/day per campaign, $20/day per ad group
- Start broad targeting with smart bidding for 2-3 days, then optimize
- Expect learning phase of 50+ conversions before stable CPA
- Scale by duplicating winning ad groups (don't increase budget more than 20% per day on performing ad groups)

## Influencer strategy on TikTok

**Creator tiers for TikTok:**
| Tier | Followers | Typical cost per post | Engagement rate | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Nano** | 1K-10K | $50-250 | 8-15% | Authenticity, niche communities, product seeding |
| **Micro** | 10K-100K | $250-2,500 | 4-8% | Best ROI for most brands, targeted reach |
| **Mid-tier** | 100K-500K | $2,500-10K | 2-5% | Scale with decent engagement |
| **Macro** | 500K-1M | $10K-25K | 1-3% | Brand awareness, wide reach |
| **Mega** | 1M+ | $25K-100K+ | 0.5-2% | Mass awareness, PR value |

**Campaign types:**
- **Product seeding** — send free product, no payment, hope for organic mention. Works best with nano/micro creators. Expect 10-20% post rate.
- **Sponsored posts** — paid content with brand messaging. Include usage rights for repurposing as Spark Ads.
- **Affiliate/commission** — creator earns per sale via tracking link. Works for ecommerce with proven conversion.
- **Brand ambassador** — ongoing relationship (3-12 months). Best for brand consistency and repeat exposure.
- **Hashtag challenge seeding** — pay 5-10 creators to kickstart a branded challenge, then let organic participation amplify.

**ROI measurement on TikTok:**
- **EMV (Earned Media Value)** — industry standard but imperfect. Typical formula: impressions × CPM benchmark
- **ROAS** — for paid campaigns and Spark Ads. Track via TikTok Pixel or CAPI (Conversions API)
- **Attribution window** — TikTok drives delayed conversions. Campaign influence extends 30-45 days post-exposure, some categories 90+ days
- **Engagement rate** — (likes + comments + shares + saves) / views. Benchmark: 4-8% for micro-influencers on TikTok
- **Brand lift** — pre/post awareness surveys for awareness campaigns
- **UTM tracking** — append UTM parameters to bio links and ad URLs. TikTok's in-app browser strips some referrer data, so use dedicated landing pages per creator

## TikTok Shop

- **Product listing ads** — appear in the Shop tab and in-feed
- **Live shopping** — real-time product selling during TikTok Lives (strong in Southeast Asia, growing in US/EU)
- **Affiliate program** — creators earn commission on Shop sales via product links
- **Shoppable videos** — tag products directly in organic or sponsored content
- **Seller Center** — manage inventory, orders, shipping from TikTok's dashboard
- **Commission structure** — TikTok takes 5% + creators earn 10-20% affiliate commission
