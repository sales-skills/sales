## Submission process

1. **Create an account** at tinylaunch.com
2. **Prepare materials** — product name, description, screenshots, working product URL
3. **Submit your product** — choose Standard (free) or Premium ($39)
4. **Wait for launch window** — products launch in weekly batches (Premium skips queue)
5. **Engage during launch week** — respond to feedback, share on social
6. **If Top 3** — receive badge + dofollow backlink (badge must embed link to TinyLaunch)

## When TinyLaunch makes sense

**Good fit:**
- You're an indie maker or early-stage startup
- You want a high-DR dofollow backlink (DR60-71 is among the strongest free directories)
- You're willing to compete for Top 3 or pay $39 for guaranteed backlink
- You want newsletter exposure to ~11,000 subscribers
- You prefer a slower weekly cadence with less competition per launch window

**Not ideal:**
- You need an instant listing — TinyLaunch uses weekly launch batches with a queue
- You need a guaranteed free backlink — free tier only gives backlinks to Top 3
- You need the highest traffic — organic traffic is modest (~87/mo per LaunchDirectories, likely undercounted)
- You're not willing to actively promote your launch during the voting window
