## Step 4 — Actionable guidance

### For users submitting (free tier)

1. Create an account at uneed.best
2. Prepare: clear product name, problem-focused tagline, high-quality screenshots, working product URL
3. Add UTM tracking: `?utm_source=uneed&utm_medium=directory&utm_campaign=launch`
4. **Join the Line** for a free automatic launch slot — you'll be assigned a date
5. **Promote during your 24-hour window** — share on X/Twitter, engage with the maker community
6. **Respond to every comment** — Uneed's community values engagement

**Note**: DR74 dofollow is among the highest free backlinks from any directory — higher than Open Launch (DR65), DevHunt (DR57-62), and MicroLaunch (DR59). Only Product Hunt (DR91), Hacker News (DR91), Indie Hackers (DR75), and F6S (DR74) match or exceed it.

### For users considering paid options

**Skip the Line ($29.99)** — choose your launch date instead of waiting:
- Worth it if you're coordinating a multi-platform launch and need control over timing
- Cheaper than most paid directory options (Open Launch $12 for skip, but Uneed has higher DR)

**Pro subscription ($89/yr)** — worth evaluating if you're a serial launcher:
- +3 vote multiplier gives a meaningful edge in daily competitions
- 1 free relaunch/mo ($15 value × 12 = $180/yr in relaunches alone)
- 1 free skip-the-line pass/yr ($29.99 value)
- Pro badge adds credibility to your profile
- Break-even after ~6 months of active use

**Newsletter sponsorship ($147)** — 15,000 subscribers at 40% open rate:
- ~6,000 opens per issue
- CPM: ~$9.80 per 1K subscribers, ~$24.50 per 1K opens
- Compare with TinyLaunch newsletter ($50 for 11K subs, 33% open rate) — Uneed is pricier but larger reach and higher open rate

### For multi-directory launchers

Uneed fits into **Phase 1 — Instant listings** if you use Skip the Line ($29.99), or **Phase 3 — Free editorial queues** for the free tier. The DR74 backlink makes it one of the most valuable first-party directory submissions — ranking above Open Launch (DR65) and below only the major third-party platforms (Product Hunt, Hacker News, Indie Hackers). Submit free on Day 1 for the queue, or pay $29.99 to coordinate timing with other launches.

## Gotchas

*Best-effort from research — review these, especially items about features and pricing that may change.*

- **Traffic numbers vary wildly.** Uneed claims 294K monthly visits; third-party sources report 2K-71K. The real number is likely somewhere in between — the site claim may include bot traffic or count differently.
- **Free queue wait is unclear.** "Join the Line" gives you an automatic slot, but the wait time isn't documented. Could be days or weeks depending on submission volume.
- **Daily competition means no guaranteed visibility.** 8-15 products compete each day. If you launch on a crowded day, you may get less engagement.
- **Pro vote multiplier is powerful.** The +3 vote multiplier means Pro users have a structural advantage in daily competitions. If you're competing on free tier, know that Pro users' votes count more.
- **Ad spots are frequently sold out.** Premium ($197/mo) and Main ($297/mo) ad spots are listed as sold out. If you want ad placement, you may need to join a waitlist.
- **Early bird Pro pricing may not last.** $89/yr is limited to 69 spots — the regular price is $120/yr.
- **Newsletter sponsorship is one-shot.** $147 gets you one newsletter issue, not recurring placement.

- **Self-improving**: If you discover something not covered here, append it to `references/learnings.md` with today's date.

## Troubleshooting

### Waiting a long time in the free queue
**Symptom**: Submitted via "Join the Line" but haven't been assigned a launch date
**Cause**: Free queue wait time depends on submission volume. With 8-15 daily launches, slots fill up.
**Solution**: If timing matters, pay $29.99 to Skip the Line and choose your date. Otherwise, wait — the free backlink is worth it (DR74). While waiting, submit to instant-listing platforms (ProductBurst, SoloPush, DevHunt) to start collecting backlinks immediately.

### Low votes during daily competition
**Symptom**: Product launched but received few votes compared to competitors
**Cause**: Daily competitions are competitive. Pro users have a +3 vote multiplier, giving them a structural advantage.
**Solution**: Promote your Uneed launch on X/Twitter during the 24-hour window. Engage with the Uneed community before your launch day — vote on other products, leave comments, build relationships. Consider Pro ($89/yr) for the vote multiplier if you plan to launch multiple products.

### Backlink not appearing in SEO tools after launch
**Symptom**: Launched on Uneed but backlink not showing in Ahrefs/Moz
**Cause**: New backlinks take time for crawlers to discover. DR74 domains get crawled frequently but not instantly.
**Solution**: Wait 2-4 weeks for crawlers to find it. Verify manually by inspecting your product page HTML on uneed.best for a dofollow link. DR74 is high enough that major crawlers will pick it up within a few weeks.
