## Submission process

1. **Create an account** at uneed.best
2. **Prepare materials** — product name, description, screenshots, working product URL
3. **Submit your product** via /submit-a-tool
4. **Choose launch option** — Join the Line (free, automatic slot) or Skip the Line ($29.99, choose date)
5. **Compete in daily voting** — 24-hour window, products ranked by upvotes
6. **Engage with community** — respond to feedback, support other launches

## When Uneed makes sense

**Good fit:**
- You want one of the highest free dofollow backlinks available (DR74)
- You're launching a tech product (SaaS, AI tool, dev tool, productivity)
- You want access to a large maker community (64,000+)
- You want newsletter exposure to 15,000 subscribers at 40% open rate
- You're comfortable competing in daily voting windows

**Not ideal:**
- You need instant listing with no queue — free tier has a wait (use ProductBurst or SoloPush instead)
- You're launching a non-tech product — Uneed focuses on tech tools
- You want guaranteed featuring without competition — daily voting means ranking matters
- You need the absolute highest DR — SideProjectors (DR69) is lower, but Product Hunt (DR91) and Hacker News (DR91) are higher
