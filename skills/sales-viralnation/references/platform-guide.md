# Viral Nation Platform Reference

## Platform overview

Viral Nation is a global social-first marketing agency and technology company founded in 2014 by Joe Gagliese and Mat Micheli. Headquartered in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada (2680 Skymark Avenue, Suite 400, L4W 5L6). Positioned as "the global leader in social-first transformation and commerce." Serves enterprise brands including Uber, Chegg, Chelsea FC, Golden Globe Awards, Ubisoft, Activision Blizzard, Urban Outfitters, Logitech, Smartwater, and Signet Jewelers. Claims 1B+ engaged customers reached, 35 industry awards, 7x average ROAS increase, and 42% average CAC reduction.

**Key differentiator**: Hybrid model — combines a full-service agency with two proprietary AI platforms (CreatorOS and Secure). This is not a self-serve SaaS you can sign up for independently; the technology is bundled with managed services.

## Key modules

### CreatorOS

End-to-end creator management and campaign analytics platform. Evolved from their earlier product, Influsoft. Launched September 2023.

**Creator discovery & management:**
- Advanced search with creator look-alike modeling
- Access to Viral Nation's exclusive talent roster + ability to invite external creators and agencies
- Consolidated creator profiles tracking historical content, ongoing projects, and campaign participation

**Campaign management:**
- Campaign creation, population, and sharing in one platform
- Task and deliverable matching with post performance metrics
- Budget tracking across multiple campaigns
- Content approval workflows
- Eliminates spreadsheet-based workflows

**Analytics & measurement:**
- Campaign media value visualizations
- Predictive attribution modeling
- Sentiment analysis
- Multi-platform performance tracking
- ROI measurement across paid, earned, and owned

**Key terminology:**
- "Campaign media value" = their proprietary metric for content value
- "Predictive attribution" = AI-modeled attribution beyond last-click
- "Creator intelligence" = their term for the AI-powered sourcing and matching engine

### Viral Nation Secure

AI-powered brand safety and creator vetting tool. Won SXSW Innovation Award. Version 2.0 released October 2024. Powered by Google Gemini.

**Content scanning:**
- Scans up to 15 years of creator content across all major social platforms
- Processing time: ~48 hours for comprehensive historical analysis
- Analyzes text, video, images, audio, transcriptions, and OCR data
- Supports 35+ languages

**Risk categories (10+):**
- Hate speech and discrimination
- Drug use and substance references
- Violence and graphic content
- Sexual content
- Political extremism
- Custom brand-specific categories

**Brand fit features:**
- Detects past paid ads, sponsorships, and unpaid brand mentions
- Content classification by topic, style, and age rating
- Customizable risk tolerance and sensitivity levels
- Creator-brand alignment scoring

**Scale:**
- Can analyze hundreds to thousands of creators simultaneously
- Multi-lingual analysis with worldwide coverage

## Workflow setup

### Typical brand campaign workflow

1. **Discovery call** — Viral Nation team assesses brand goals, target audience, budget, timeline
2. **Strategy & proposal** — Custom campaign plan with creator recommendations, content strategy, distribution plan
3. **Creator vetting** — Secure scans shortlisted creators for brand safety (48-hour turnaround per batch)
4. **Creator selection & negotiation** — Team handles outreach, contracts, rate negotiation
5. **Content creation** — Creators produce content; approval workflow via CreatorOS
6. **Distribution** — Content published on creator channels + optional paid amplification
7. **Reporting** — Performance analytics via CreatorOS: reach, engagement, attribution, ROI

### Secure vetting workflow

1. **Configure risk tolerance** — Set sensitivity levels across 10+ risk categories
2. **Submit creator list** — Upload creators to scan (can handle hundreds simultaneously)
3. **AI analysis** — Gemini-powered scanning of historical content (text, video, image, audio)
4. **Review flagged content** — Dashboard shows flagged items with risk level and context
5. **Make decisions** — Accept, reject, or flag creators for manual review
6. **Ongoing monitoring** — Continuous screening after partnership begins

### When Viral Nation is the right choice

- You're an enterprise brand ($50K+ campaigns) wanting full agency execution
- Brand safety is a top priority (regulated industry, risk-averse brand)
- You need multi-market campaigns across 30+ countries
- You want talent representation AND brand campaign services from one provider
- You need TikTok-specific expertise with dedicated strategy

### When to look elsewhere

- Budget under $50K — no self-serve option; consider Modash, Heepsy, or HypeAuditor
- Need API access for custom integrations — consider NeoReach or CreatorIQ
- Want to manage campaigns yourself with platform tooling — consider Aspire, Modash, or influData
- Need transparent, published pricing — consider Modash ($199/mo), HypeAuditor ($299/mo), or Open Influence ($50-500/mo)
- Only need brand safety scanning — evaluate CreatorScore or HypeAuditor (both available without managed services)
